## 🔬 Theoretical Foundations | 理论基础

### 1. Phenomenological Method | 现象学方法

**Key Concepts | 核心概念**:
- **Intentionality | 意向性**: Consciousness is always consciousness *of* something / 意识总是关于某物的意识
- **First-Person Method | 第一人称方法**: Experience studied from the lived perspective / 从活过的视角研究体验
- **Life-World | 生活世界**: Cultural and practical context of experience / 体验的文化和实践语境

**Application | 应用**: Enhanced emotion analysis with intentional structure mapping / 通过意向结构映射增强情绪分析

---

### 2. Temporal Consciousness | 时间意识

**Husserl's Triadic Structure | 胡塞尔三重结构**:

```
          Primal Impression (原印象)
                 ↓
    Retention ←──┼──→ Protention
    (滞留)       │      (前摄)
   just-past     │   anticipated-future
                 │
            [Specious Present ~2-3s]
            [显似现在 ~2-3 秒]
```

**Emotion-Time Mapping | 情绪 - 时间映射**:
- **Fear | 恐惧**: Protention-dominant (threat anticipation) / 前摄主导（威胁预期）
- **Sadness | 悲伤**: Retention-dominant (loss-focused) / 滞留主导（损失聚焦）
- **Joy | 喜悦**: Primal impression-dominant (present engagement) / 原印象主导（当下投入）

### 4. Embodied Ecological Psychology | 具身生态心理学

**Gibson's Affordances | 吉布森功能可供性**:

- Direct perception without inference / 无需推理的直接知觉
