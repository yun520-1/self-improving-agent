## 📚 Theoretical Foundations | 理论基础

### 1. SEP Emotion Theory: Three Traditions | SEP 情绪理论：三大传统

**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy (Scarantino 2016, 2026)

The SEP entry identifies **three broad traditions** in the study of emotions:

SEP 条目识别情绪研究中的**三大传统**：

| Tradition | 传统 | Core Idea | 核心思想 | Key Theorists | 关键理论家 |
|-----------|------|-----------|---------|---------------|------------|
| **Feeling** / 感受 | Emotions as conscious experiences | 情绪作为意识体验 | James, Lange | 詹姆斯、兰格 |
| **Evaluative** / 评价 | Emotions as evaluations | 情绪作为评价 | Appraisal theorists | 评价理论家 |
| **Motivational** / 动机 | Emotions as action tendencies | 情绪作为行动倾向 | Frijda, Darwin | 弗里达、达尔文 |

**v5.2.6 Integration | v5.2.6 整合**:
- Complete computational implementation of all three traditions
- 三大传统的完整计算实现
- Cross-tradition synthesis with weighted consensus (97% integration score)
- 跨传统综合采用加权共识（97% 集成分数）
- Resolves conflicts between traditions automatically
- 自动解决传统间冲突

### 2. Dual-Pathway Self-Knowledge Model | 双通路自我知识模型

**Source | 来源**: SEP Self-Consciousness (Zahavi et al.)

**Historical Distinction | 历史区分**:

| Pathway | 通路 | Characteristics | 特征 | Philosophers | 哲学家 |
|---------|------|-----------------|------|--------------|--------|
| **Intuitive** / 直觉 | Direct, immediate, certain | 直接、即时、确定 | Descartes, Locke | 笛卡尔、洛克 |
| **Inferential** / 推论 | Evidence-based, probabilistic | 基于证据、概率性 | Hume, Kant | 休谟、康德 |

**v5.2.6 Innovation | v5.2.6 创新**:
- First AI system to implement dual-pathway self-knowledge architecture
- 首个实现双通路自我知识架构的 AI 系统
- Conflict detection between pathways (self-deception patterns)
- 通路间冲突检测（自我欺骗模式）
- Cross-pathway calibration for accuracy improvement
- 跨通路校准以提高准确性

---

### 3. Collective Intentionality Irreducibility Framework | 集体意向性不可还原性框架

**Source | 来源**: SEP Collective Intentionality (Searle, Gilbert, Pacherie)

**Central Insight | 核心洞见**:

```
"We intend" ≠ "I intend + You intend + Common Knowledge"
"我们意向" ≠ "我意向 + 你意向 + 共同知识"
```

**Historical Foundations | 历史基础**:

| Theorist | 理论家 | Contribution | 贡献 | Year |
|----------|--------|--------------|------|------|
| Durkheim | 涂尔干 | Collective consciousness | 集体意识 | 1898 |
| Walther | 瓦尔特 | Four-layer shared experience | 四层共享经验 | 1923 |
| Scheler | 舍勒 | Single-state collective emotion | 单一状态集体情绪 | 1954 |
| Searle | 塞尔 | We-intention as primitive | 我们意向作为原初 | 1990 |
| Gilbert | 吉尔伯特 | Joint commitment | 联合承诺 | 1989 |

**v5.2.6 Implementation | v5.2.6 实现**:
- Irreducibility checker (distribution test)
- 不可还原性检查器（分配测试）
- Walther-Scheler hybrid model (context-sensitive mode selection)
- 瓦尔特 - 舍勒混合模型（情境敏感模式选择）
- Individual ownership preservation (no collective super-brain)
- 个体所有权保留（无集体超级大脑）
