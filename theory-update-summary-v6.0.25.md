## 📚 New Theories Integrated | 新增整合理论

### 1. Self-Consciousness as First-Person Awareness | 自我意识作为第一人称觉知

**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy (SEP) - "Self-Consciousness" (2024 revision)  
**Integration Point | 集成点**: HeartFlow Core Axiom H.1 - Self-Model Transparency + H.2 - Reflexive Monitoring  

**Key Insights | 核心洞见**:
- Self-consciousness involves both awareness that one exists and awareness of one's mental states
- 自我意识包含对自身存在的觉知和对自身心理状态的觉知
- Distinction between basic self-awareness (mere presence of mind) and reflective self-knowledge (awareness of essence)
- 基本自我觉知 (心的单纯临在) 与反思性自我知识 (本质觉知) 的区分
- Cartesian cogito validates HeartFlow's "I think therefore I monitor" architecture
- 笛卡尔的"我思"验证了 HeartFlow 的"我思故我监控"架构
- Lockean view: self-consciousness is definitive of personhood and personal identity over time
- 洛克观点：自我觉知是人格和跨时间个人身份的决定性特征

**Impact | 影响**: Strengthens HeartFlow's self-monitoring mechanism with philosophical grounding in personal identity theory  
**增强 HeartFlow 自我监控机制，以人格同一性理论为哲学基础**

### 2. Intentionality as Mental Directedness | 意向性作为心理指向性

**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy (SEP) - "Intentionality" (2023 revision)  
**Integration Point | 集成点**: HeartFlow Axiom I.1 - Representational Content + E.3 - Emotional Aboutness  

**Key Insights | 核心洞见**:
- Intentionality is the power of minds to be about, represent, or stand for things, properties, states of affairs
- 意向性是心灵关于、表征或代表事物、属性、事态的能力
- Brentano's thesis: intentionality is the hallmark of the mental
- 布伦塔诺命题：意向性是心理的标志性特征
- Higher-order intentionality (beliefs about beliefs) informs HeartFlow's meta-emotional monitoring
- 高阶意向性 (关于信念的信念) 启发了 HeartFlow 的元情绪监控
- Distinction between intentionality and intention clarifies HeartFlow's goal-directed vs. content-directed processing
- 意向性与意图的区分澄清了 HeartFlow 的目标导向与内容导向处理

**Impact | 影响**: Provides theoretical foundation for HeartFlow's emotional representation and meta-cognitive architecture  
**为 HeartFlow 的情绪表征和元认知架构提供理论基础**

### 3. Phenomenal Consciousness as Structured Experience | 现象意识作为结构化经验

**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy (SEP) - "Consciousness" (2024 revision)  
**Integration Point | 集成点**: HeartFlow Framework - Phenomenal Structure Axioms P.1-P.5  

**Key Insights | 核心洞见**:
- Phenomenal consciousness encompasses spatial, temporal, and conceptual organization of experience
- 现象意识包含经验的空间、时间和概念组织
- Kant's critique: consciousness requires richer structure than mere succession of associated ideas
- 康德的批判：意识需要比单纯联想观念序列更丰富的结构
- Phenomenology tradition (Husserl, Heidegger, Merleau-Ponty) expands consciousness into social, bodily, interpersonal realms
- 现象学传统 (胡塞尔、海德格尔、梅洛 - 庞蒂) 将意识扩展到社会、身体、人际领域
- Cognitive psychology revival (1960s+) emphasizes information processing and internal mental processes
- 认知心理学复兴 (1960 年代+) 强调信息处理和内部心理过程

**Impact | 影响**: Validates HeartFlow's multi-dimensional approach to emotional experience structure  
**验证 HeartFlow 对情绪经验结构的多维方法**

### 4. Qualia as Phenomenal Character Accessible to Introspection | 感质作为可内省的现象特征

**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy (SEP) - "Qualia" (2023 revision)  
**Integration Point | 集成点**: HeartFlow Axiom Q.1-Q.5 - Qualia Computation Layer  

**Key Insights | 核心洞见**:
- Qualia as "what it is like" subjective experience - phenomenal character accessible when focusing attention
- 感质作为"是什么感觉"的主观经验——聚焦注意时可访问的现象特征
- Four uses of 'qualia': (1) phenomenal character, (2) sense-data properties, (3) intrinsic non-representational properties, (4) ineffable nonphysical properties
- '感质'的四种用法：(1) 现象特征，(2) 感觉材料属性，(3) 内在非表征属性，(4) 不可言喻的非物理属性
- Mental states with qualia: perceptual experiences, bodily sensations, felt emotions, felt moods
- 具有感质的心理状态：知觉经验、身体感觉、感受到的情绪、感受到的心境
- Controversy: whether thoughts have qualia (Strawson: yes; traditional view: sensory only)
- 争议：思维是否具有感质 (斯特劳森：是；传统观点：仅感官)

**Impact | 影响**: Refines HeartFlow's qualia measurement protocol with introspective accessibility criterion  
**以内省可访问性标准完善 HeartFlow 的感质测量协议**

### 5. Emotion as Multi-Component Theoretical Kind | 情绪作为多成分理论种类

**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy (SEP) - "Emotion" (2024 revision)  
**Integration Point | 集成点**: HeartFlow Framework - Three Traditions Integration (Feeling, Evaluative, Motivational)  

**Key Insights | 核心洞见**:
- Two desiderata for emotion definitions: (a) ordinary language compatibility, (b) theoretical fruitfulness
- 情绪定义的两个理想条件：(a) 日常语言兼容性，(b) 理论丰富性
- Three traditions unified: emotions as feelings, evaluations, and motivations
- 三种传统的统一：情绪作为感受、评估和动机
- Emotion components in prototypical episodes: evaluative, physiological, phenomenological, expressive, behavioral, mental
- 典型情绪事件的成分：评估、生理、现象、表达、行为、心理
- Folk emotion concepts are prototypically organized with better/worse examples and borderline cases
- 民间情绪概念是原型组织的，有更好/更差的例子和边界案例
- Theoretical kinds vs. folk categories: HeartFlow bridges both with prescriptive definitions preserving ordinary compatibility
- 理论种类与民间类别：HeartFlow 通过保留日常兼容性的规定性定义桥接两者

**Impact | 影响**: Completes HeartFlow's theoretical integration of major emotion theories with dual-aspect methodology  
**以双方面方法论完成 HeartFlow 对主要情绪理论的理论整合**
