### 2. Consciousness Neuroscience (NCC) | 意识神经相关

**Theoretical Framework**:
- **NCC (Neural Correlates of Consciousness)**: Minimal neural mechanisms sufficient for conscious experience
- **GWT (Global Workspace Theory)**: Consciousness as global information broadcasting
- **IIT (Integrated Information Theory)**: Consciousness as integrated information (Φ)

**Key Insights**:
1. **NCC Research**
   - Visual consciousness: V1, V2, V4, IT cortex
   - Auditory consciousness: A1, A2, auditory association
   - Prefrontal cortex: Higher-order consciousness
   - Thalamocortical loops: Consciousness integration

2. **GWT (Baars, Dehaene)**
   - Unconscious processing: Parallel, modular
   - Conscious processing: Serial, global broadcast
   - Attention gateway: Selective information access
   - Working memory integration

3. **IIT (Tononi)**
   - Φ (Phi): Integrated information measure
   - Axioms: Existence, composition, information, integration, exclusion
   - Postulates: Mechanism, composition, differentiation, integration
   - Prediction: Any system with Φ>0 has consciousness

### 3. Computational Neuroscience | 计算神经科学

**Core Frameworks**:
1. **Neural Coding**
   - Rate coding: Information in firing rate
   - Temporal coding: Information in spike timing
   - Population coding: Information in ensemble activity
   - Sparse coding: Efficient representation

2. **Synaptic Plasticity**
   - Hebbian learning: "Cells that fire together, wire together"
   - LTP (Long-Term Potentiation): Synaptic strengthening
   - LTD (Long-Term Depression): Synaptic weakening
   - STDP (Spike-Timing-Dependent Plasticity)

3. **Network Dynamics**
   - Attractor networks: Stable activity patterns
   - Oscillations: Rhythmic synchronization
   - Chaos theory: Sensitive dependence on initial conditions
   - Criticality: Edge of chaos optimization
