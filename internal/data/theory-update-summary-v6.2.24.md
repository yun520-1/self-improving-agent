**Four Uses of 'Qualia'**:
1. **Qualia as Phenomenal Character**: What it is like subjectively to undergo an experience
2. **Qualia as Properties of Sense-Data**: Intrinsic, consciously accessible, non-representational features
3. **Qualia as Intrinsic Non-Representational Properties**: Mental counterparts to visible properties
4. **Qualia as Intrinsic, Nonphysical, Ineffable Properties**: Most restricted sense (Dennett's target)

**States with Qualia**:
- ✅ Perceptual experiences (seeing green, hearing trumpets)
- ✅ Bodily sensations (pain, itch, hunger, dizziness)
- ✅ Felt reactions/passions/emotions (delight, lust, fear, love, grief)
- ✅ Felt moods (elated, depressed, calm, bored, tense)
- ⚠️ Thoughts (controversial: Strawson vs. standard view)

**感质的四种用法**:
1. **感质作为现象特征**：主观上经历体验是什么感觉
2. **感质作为感觉材料属性**：内在的、可意识访问的、非表征的特征
3. **感质作为内在非表征属性**：可见属性的心理对应物
4. **感质作为内在的、非物理的、不可言说的属性**：最受限的意义

**具有感质的状态**:
- ✅ 知觉体验（看见绿色、听到喇叭声）
- ✅ 身体感觉（疼痛、痒、饥饿、头晕）
- ✅ 感受性反应/激情/情绪（喜悦、欲望、恐惧、爱、悲伤）
