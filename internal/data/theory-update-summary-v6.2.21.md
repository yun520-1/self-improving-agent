## 3. Self-Consciousness Architecture v4 | 自我意识架构 v4

### Source | 来源
- SEP: "Self-Consciousness" (2024 edition)
- Historical: Aristotle, Augustine, Avicenna, Aquinas, Descartes, Locke, Hume, Kant, Fichte, Husserl, Sartre
- Contemporary: Strawson 1959, Evans 1980, Cassam 1997, Zahavi 2005-2024

### Levels of Self-Consciousness | 自我意识层次

| Level | Description | Historical Source | Chinese |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|---------|
| **Pre-reflective** | Immediate, non-objectifying self-awareness | Heidelberg School, Sartre | 前反思 - 直接的、非对象化的自我意识 |
| **Reflective** | Taking self as object of awareness | Descartes, Locke | 反思 - 将自我作为意识对象 |
| **Conceptual** | Self-awareness using concepts ("I") | Kant's "I think" | 概念 - 使用概念（"我"）的自我意识 |
| **Narrative** | Self as temporally extended story | Locke's personal identity | 叙事 - 自我作为时间延展的故事 |

### Key Insights | 核心洞见

#### Cartesian Cogito | 笛卡尔的"我思"
- "I am, I exist" is necessarily true when conceived
- Foundation: awareness that one is thinking + awareness that one exists
- **Limitation**: Assumes substantial self (critiqued by Hume, Kant)

#### Humean Challenge | 休谟挑战
- "I never can catch myself at any time without a perception"
- Self is "heap or collection of different perceptions"
- **Response**: Pre-reflective self-awareness doesn't require impression of self

#### Kantian Apperception | 康德统觉
- "I think" must accompany all my representations
- Purely formal representation (not intuition of self as object)
- Required for unity of conscious experience over time

#### Embodied Agency | 具身能动性
- Self-consciousness requires conception of oneself as embodied agent
- Located in objective world structured by space, time, causality
- **Source**: Strawson 1959, Evans 1980, Cassam 1997
