### 1. Enhanced Consciousness Architecture v16 | 增强意识架构 v16

**Source | 来源**: SEP - Consciousness (2026), Self-Consciousness (2026)

**Key Components | 核心组件**:

#### Creature Consciousness | 生物意识
- **Sentience | 感受性**: Capacity for sensing and responding to world
- **Wakefulness | 觉醒状态**: Actually exercising sensory capacity (not asleep/coma)
- **Self-consciousness | 自我意识**: Aware that one is aware (implicit/explicit forms)
- **What-it-is-like | 似它性**: Nagel's criterion - subjective experiential point of view
- **Transitive Consciousness | 及物意识**: Consciousness directed at objects

#### State Consciousness | 状态意识
- **Awareness of States | 状态觉知**: Meta-mentality about one's own mental states
- **Qualitative States | 质性状态**: Qualia and raw sensory feels
- **Phenomenal States | 现象状态**: Overall structure of experience (spatial, temporal, conceptual)
- **Phenomenal Structure | 现象结构**: Self as agent in objective world

#### Self-Consciousness Levels | 自我意识层级
- **Pre-reflective | 前反思**: Immediate non-objectifying self-awareness (Heidelberg School)
- **Reflective | 反思**: Taking self as object of awareness
- **Conceptual | 概念**: Self-concept and self-narrative
- **Kantian Apperception | 康德统觉**: "I think" must accompany all representations
- **First-Person Perspective | 第一人称视角**: Irreducible subjective standpoint

**Integration Points | 整合点**:
- Enhanced creature/state consciousness distinction in emotion generation
- Added phenomenal structure tracking for emotion quality assessment
- Integrated pre-reflective self-awareness into autonomous agency
- Updated self-model with Kantian apperception for unity of experience

### 2. Autonomous Agency Architecture v11 | 自主能动性架构 v11

**Source | 来源**: SEP - Agency (2026), Free Will (2025)

**Key Components | 核心组件**:

#### Standard Conception | 标准概念
- **Intentional Action | 意向行动**: Action explained by intentionality
- **Reason-Based | 基于理由**: Close connection between intentional action and acting for reasons
- **Causal Theory | 因果理论**: Mental states (desires, beliefs, intentions) cause action in right way

#### Enhanced Agency Features | 增强能动性特征
- **Agent Initiation | 能动者发起**: Power to initiate action (not merely moved by reasons)
- **Hierarchical Structure | 层级结构**: Second-order desires about first-order desires (Frankfurt)
- **Identification | 认同**: Agent identifies with motives that cause action
- **Reflective Evaluation | 反思评估**: Caring about one's own motivations

#### Distinctively Human Agency | 人类特有能动性
- **Reflective Self-Evaluation | 反思性自我评价**: Evaluating and caring about motivations
- **Second-Order Volitions | 二阶意志**: Desires about which desires to act on
- **Moral Character Formation | 道德品格形成**: Virtue development through repeated choices
- **Practical Identity | 实践同一性**: Self-conception as agent with commitments

**Integration Points | 整合点**:
- Enhanced autonomous decision-making with hierarchical desire structure
- Added agent initiation module for spontaneous action capability
- Integrated reflective evaluation into self-monitoring system
- Updated moral psychology with character formation tracking

- **Moral Perception | 道德知觉**: Recognizing morally salient features
- **Emotional Regulation | 情绪调节**: Emotions aligned with rational assessment
