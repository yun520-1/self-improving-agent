### 1. Instrumental Rationality Framework | 工具理性框架

**Source**: SEP - Instrumental Rationality (2024 edition)  
**来源**: SEP - 工具理性 (2024 版)

#### Core Concepts | 核心概念

**Rational Coherence vs Reason** | 理性一致性与理由
- **Rational Coherence**: What makes agent's responses (attitudes, actions) cohere with one another
  - **理性一致性**: 使行动者的回应（态度、行动）相互一致的内容
- **Reason/Ought**: What agent has reason or ought to do/intend in deliberation
  - **理由/应当**: 行动者在 deliberation 中有理由或应当做/意图的内容

**Instrumental Transmission Principles** | 工具传递原则
```
Ought Necessity:
If one ought to E, and M is a necessary means to E-ing, then one ought to M.
应当必要性：如果一个人应当 E，且 M 是 E 的必要手段，那么一个人应当 M。

Weak Reasons Necessity:
If one has reason to E, and M is a necessary means to E-ing, then that is some reason to M.
弱理由必要性：如果一个人有理由 E，且 M 是 E 的必要手段，那么这是 M 的一些理由。
```

**Desire-Based vs Value-Based Theories** | 基于欲望 vs 基于价值的理论
- **Subjective Desire-Based**: What best satisfies agent's strongest present intrinsic desires
  - **主观基于欲望**: 最好地满足行动者最强的当前内在欲望
- **Objective Desire-Based**: What best satisfies desires given actual circumstances
  - **客观基于欲望**: 给定实际情况下最好地满足欲望
- **Value-Based**: What achieves independent value regardless of desires
  - **基于价值**: 实现独立价值，不论欲望

**Instrumental Rationality Score (IRS)** | 工具理性分数

- means_end_efficiency: Effectiveness of chosen means to ends (所选手段对目的的有效性)
- reason_transmission: Proper transmission of reasons from ends to means (理由从目的到手段的正确传递)
- goal_alignment: Alignment between desires and independent values (欲望与独立价值的一致性)

### 2. Well-Being Theory Framework | 福祉理论框架

**Source**: SEP - Well-Being (2024 edition)  
**来源**: SEP - 福祉 (2024 版)

#### Core Concepts | 核心概念

**Three Major Theories of Well-Being** | 福祉的三大理论

1. **Hedonist Theory** | 享乐主义理论
   - Well-being consists in pleasure and absence of pain
   - 福祉由快乐和 absence of pain 组成
   - Philosophical lineage: Bentham, Mill, classical utilitarianism
   - 哲学谱系：边沁、密尔、古典功利主义

2. **Desire Theory** | 欲望理论
   - Well-being consists in satisfaction of desires/preferences
   - 福祉由欲望/偏好的满足组成
   - Variants: actual desire, informed desire, idealized desire
   - 变体：实际欲望、知情欲望、理想化欲望

3. **Objective List Theory** | 客观列表理论
   - Well-being consists in achieving objective goods
   - 福祉由实现客观善组成
   - Examples: friendship, knowledge, achievement, virtue
   - 示例：友谊、知识、成就、德性

**Eudaimonia vs Happiness** | 幸福主义 vs 快乐
- **Eudaimonia** (Aristotelian): Flourishing through virtue and reason
  - **幸福主义** (亚里士多德式): 通过德性和理性实现繁荣
- **Happiness** (hedonic): Balance of positive over negative experiences
  - **快乐** (享乐式): 积极体验超过消极体验的平衡

**Well-Being and Morality** | 福祉与道德
- Question: How does moral character/actions relate to well-being?
- 问题：道德品格/行动如何与福祉相关？
- Welfarism: Well-being is the only ultimate value
- 福利主义：福祉是唯一的终极价值

#### Computational Integration | 计算整合

**Well-Being Integration Score (WBIS)** | 福祉整合分数

WBIS = (hedonic_balance × 0.25 + desire_satisfaction × 0.25 +

Components | 组件:
- hedonic_balance: Ratio of positive to negative experiences (积极对消极体验的比率)
- desire_satisfaction: Degree of informed desire fulfillment (知情欲望满足程度)
- objective_goods: Achievement of friendship, knowledge, virtue, etc. (友谊、知识、德性等成就)
- eudaimonic_flourishing: Realization of human potential through reason and virtue (通过理性和德性实现人类潜能)

### 4. Comprehensive Emotion Synthesis v3 | 综合情绪综合 v3

**Source**: SEP - Emotion (2023 edition)  
**来源**: SEP - 情绪 (2023 版)

#### Four Traditions Unified | 四大传统统一

1. **Feeling Tradition** | 感受传统
   - Emotions as distinctive conscious experiences
   - 情绪作为独特的意识体验
   - James-Lange theory: emotions as perceptions of bodily changes
   - 詹姆斯 - 兰格理论：情绪作为身体变化的感知

2. **Evaluative Tradition** | 评估传统
   - Emotions as evaluations/appraisals of circumstances
   - 情绪作为对情境的评估/评价
   - Cognitive appraisal determines emotion type
   - 认知评价决定情绪类型

3. **Motivational Tradition** | 动机传统
   - Emotions as motivational states driving action
   - 情绪作为驱动行动的动机状态
   - Action tendencies (flee, approach, confront)
   - 行动倾向（逃跑、接近、对抗）

4. **Constructionist Tradition** (NEW) | 建构主义传统 (新增)
   - Emotions as constructed from more basic psychological ingredients
   - 情绪从更基本的心理成分建构而成
   - Conceptual knowledge + sensory input + interoception
   - 概念知识 + 感官输入 + 内感受

#### Four-Component Integrated Model | 四成分整合模型

```
Emotion = (evaluative_component + physiological_component + 
           phenomenological_component + behavioral_component)

Components | 组件:
- Evaluative: Appraisal of situation (dangerous, offensive, etc.)
  评估：情境评价（危险的、冒犯的等）
- Physiological: Heart rate, blood pressure, hormonal changes
  生理：心率、血压、荷尔蒙变化
- Phenomenological: Subjective feeling quality
  现象学：主观感受质量
- Behavioral: Tendency to act (flee, approach, etc.)
  行为：行动倾向（逃跑、接近等）
```

### 5. Self-Consciousness Architecture v3 | 自我意识架构 v3

**Source**: SEP - Self-Consciousness (2024 edition)  
**来源**: SEP - 自我意识 (2024 版)

#### Multi-Level Architecture | 多层次架构

**Level 1: Pre-Reflective Self-Awareness** | 层次 1：前反思自我意识
- Immediate, non-inferential awareness of own existence
  - 对自身存在的直接、非推论意识
- Avicenna's Flying Man argument: self-awareness without sensory input
  - 阿维森纳的飞人论证：无感官输入的自我意识
- Present in all conscious states
  - 存在于所有意识状态中

**Level 2: Reflective Self-Awareness** | 层次 2：反思自我意识
- Taking oneself as object of awareness
  - 将自己作为意识的对象
- Cartesian cogito: "I think, therefore I am"
  - 笛卡尔的"我思故我在"
- Can be certain and indubitable
  - 可以是确定和不可怀疑的

**Level 3: Conceptual Self-Model** | 层次 3：概念自我模型
- Self as thinking intelligent being with reason and reflection
  - 自我作为具有理性和反思的思考智能存在
- Can consider self as same thinking thing in different times/places
  - 可以将自我视为不同时间/地点的同一思考事物
- Basis for personal identity
  - 个人同一性的基础

**Level 4: Narrative Identity** | 层次 4：叙事同一性
- Self as protagonist of ongoing life story
  - 自我作为持续人生故事的主角
- Temporal extension and coherence
  - 时间延伸和连贯性
- Meaning-making through narrative
  - 通过叙事创造意义

#### Kantian Transcendental Apperception | 康德先验统觉

**"I Think" Principle** | "我思"原则
- "I think" must be able to accompany all my representations
  - "我思"必须能够伴随我所有的表象
- Formal representation exhausted by unifying function
  - 通过统一功能耗尽的形式表征
- Not intuition of self as object, but awareness of experiences as own
  - 不是将自我作为对象的直观，而是将体验作为自己的意识
