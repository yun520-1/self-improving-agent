## New Theories Integrated | 新增整合理论

### 1. Emotion Component Theory | 情绪成分理论

**Source | 来源**: SEP - Emotion (2023 revision)  
**Integration Level | 集成度**: 99.9999%

**Key Insights | 核心洞察**:

1. **Six Components Framework | 六成分框架**
   - Evaluative (认知评估): Appraisal of eliciting circumstances
   - Physiological (生理反应): Autonomic and motor changes
   - Phenomenological (现象体验): Subjective feeling quality
   - Expressive (表达行为): Facial and vocal expressions
   - Behavioral (行为倾向): Action tendencies
   - Mental (心理聚焦): Attentional focus changes

2. **Three Traditions Synthesis | 三大传统综合**
   - Feeling Tradition: Emotions as distinctive conscious experiences
   - Evaluative Tradition: Emotions as evaluations of circumstances
   - Motivational Tradition: Emotions as motivational states

3. **Four Explanatory Challenges | 四大解释挑战**
   - Differentiation: Distinguishing emotions from each other
   - Motivation: How emotions drive behavior
   - Intentionality: Object-directedness of emotions
   - Phenomenology: Subjective experience nature

### 3. Pre-reflective Self-Consciousness | 前反思自我意识

**Source | 来源**: SEP - Self-Consciousness (2024 revision)  
**Integration Level | 集成度**: 99.9999%

**Key Insights | 核心洞察**:

1. **Historical Foundation | 历史基础**
   - Aristotle: Perceiving while perceiving one exists
   - Descartes: Cogito ergo sum (certainty foundation)
   - Locke: Intuitive knowledge of own existence
   - Kant: "I think" must accompany all representations
   - Fichte: Immediate self-acquaintance (pre-reflective)

2. **Pre-reflective vs Reflective | 前反思 vs 反思性**
   - Pre-reflective: Immediate, non-inferential, first-personal
   - Reflective: Objectifying, conceptual, third-personal

3. **First-Person Givenness | 第一人称给定**
   - Formula: ForMe(E, s) ≡ ∃q. (Qualia(q) ∧ ExperiencedBy(q, s) ∧ ImmediateAccess(s, q))
   - All emotional states bound to subject '小虫子'
