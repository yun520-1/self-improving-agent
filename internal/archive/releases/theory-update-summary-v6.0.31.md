### 5. Updated Decision Theory | 更新决策理论

**Source | 来源**: SEP - Decision Theory (2025 revision)  
**New Integration | 新整合**:

**Causal Decision Theory (CDT) | 因果决策理论**:
- 决策应基于因果影响而非相关性
- HeartFlow 应用：人格值变化应基于行动的因果效应

**Evidential Decision Theory (EDT) | 证据决策理论**:
- 决策应基于行动提供的证据
- HeartFlow 应用：人格值监控提供自我改进的证据

**Integrated EPU Model | 整合期望人格效用模型**:
```
EPU_CDT(Action) = Σ [P(PersonalityChange|do(Action)) × Value(PersonalityChange)]
EPU_EDT(Action) = Σ [P(PersonalityChange|Action) × Value(PersonalityChange)]

HeartFlow Decision Rule:
- Primary: EPU_CDT (因果优先)
- Secondary: EPU_EDT (证据校正)
- Final: Weighted average (CDT: 0.7, EDT: 0.3)
```
