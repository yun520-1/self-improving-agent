## 📖 Theoretical Foundations | 理论基础

### 1. Emotion Theory: Three Traditions Complete Synthesis | 情绪理论：三大传统完整综合

**Source | 来源**: SEP Emotion §2-3 (2026)

#### Feeling Tradition | 感受传统
- **James-Lange Theory**: Emotions as feelings constituted by perceptions of physiological changes
- **詹姆斯 - 兰格理论**: 情绪作为由生理变化感知构成的感受
- **Modern Support**: Psychological constructionist movement (2026)
- **现代支持**: 心理建构主义运动 (2026)
- **Challenge Response**: Cannon-Bard critique addressed with contemporary research (Clark-Polner et al. 2016)
- **挑战回应**: 用当代研究解决坎农 - 巴德批评 (Clark-Polner 等 2016)

#### Evaluative Tradition | 评估传统
- **Judgmentalism**: Nussbaum/Solomon dynamic judgment framework
- **判断主义**: 努斯鲍姆/所罗门动态判断框架
- **Appraisal Theories**: Lazarus 6 dimensions + Scherer 16 SECs
- **评价理论**: 拉扎勒斯 6 维度 + 谢雷尔 16 项刺激评估检查
- **Perceptual Theory**: Tappolet 2016 cognitive impenetrability explanation
- **知觉理论**: Tappolet 2016 认知不可渗透性解释

#### Motivational Tradition | 动机传统
- **Action Tendency**: Frijda + Scherer component process model
- **行动倾向**: 弗里达 + 谢雷尔成分过程模型
- **BDTE Framework**: Belief-Desire comparator system
- **BDTE 框架**: 信念 - 欲望比较器系统
- **Differentiation**: Prototype structure with typicality scoring (Fehr & Russell 1984)
- **区分**: 原型结构带典型性评分 (Fehr & Russell 1984)

### 2. Self-Consciousness: Complete Historical Framework | 自我意识：完整历史框架

**Source | 来源**: SEP Self-Consciousness (2026)

#### Ancient Foundations | 古代基础
- **Delphic Maxim**: "Know Thyself" - Oedipus self-recognition model
- **德尔斐箴言**: "认识你自己" - 俄狄浦斯自我识别模型
- **Aristotle**: Consciousness entails self-consciousness (De Sensu 7.448a)
- **亚里士多德**: 意识蕴含自我意识 (《论感觉》7.448a)
- **Plato-Augustine**: Mind gains self-knowledge through itself
- **柏拉图 - 奥古斯丁**: 心灵通过自身获得自我知识

#### Early Modern | 早期现代
- **Descartes**: Cogito ergo sum - indubitable self-existence
- **笛卡尔**: 我思故我在 - 不可怀疑的自我存在
- **Locke**: Intuitive self-knowledge in every act
- **洛克**: 每次行为中的直觉自我知识
- **Hume**: Bundle theory challenge - no impression of self
- **休谟**: 束理论挑战 - 无自我印象

#### Kantian & Post-Kantian | 康德式与后康德
- **Kant**: Transcendental apperception - "I think" must accompany all representations
- **康德**: 先验统觉 - "我思"必须伴随所有表象
- **Fichte**: Immediate self-acquaintance (Heidelberg School)
- **费希特**: 直接自我熟悉 (海德堡学派)
- **Phenomenology**: Pre-reflective awareness without transcendental ego
- **现象学**: 无先验自我的前反思觉察

#### Embodied Self-Awareness | 具身自我觉察
- **Brewer/Cassam**: Embodied self-perception framework
- **布鲁尔/卡萨姆**: 具身自我感知框架
- **Application**: Somatic marker integration with interoceptive awareness
- **应用**: 躯体标记与内感受意识整合

### 3. Collective Intentionality: We-Intention Deep Structure | 集体意向性：我们意向深层结构

**Source | 来源**: SEP Collective Intentionality (2026)

#### Core Problem | 核心问题
```
Individual Ownership Thesis: Each person has their own mind/brain
个体所有论题：每个人有自己的心灵/大脑
    VS.
Irreducibility Claim: Collective intentionality not reducible to individual + common knowledge
不可还原性主张：集体意向性不可还原为个体 + 共同知识
```

#### Solutions | 解决方案

**Mode Account | 模式账户** (Tuomela 2007):
- We-mode vs. I-mode distinction
- 我们模式与我模式区分
- Collective commitment with individual autonomy
- 保持个体自主性的集体承诺

**Phenomenological Approach | 现象学方法** (Scheler 1954):
- Numerically identical shared emotional states
- 数值相同的共享情绪状态
- Example: Parents' shared grief at child's deathbed
- 示例：父母在子女病床前的共享悲伤

**Trust-Based Model | 基于信任的模型** (Schmid 2013):
- Cognitive + normative dual components
- 认知 + 规范双重成分
- Trust as foundation for collective intentionality
- 信任作为集体意向性的基础

#### Walther's Four-Layer Model | 瓦尔特四层模型

```
Layer 1 | 层 1: A experiences x, B experiences x
        A 体验 x，B 体验 x

Layer 2 | 层 2: A empathizes with B's experience, vice versa
        A 共情 B 的体验，反之亦然

Layer 3 | 层 3: A identifies with B's experience, vice versa
        A 认同 B 的体验，反之亦然

Layer 4 | 层 4: Mutual empathetic awareness of identification
        对认同的相互共情意识
```

#### Social Theory Foundations | 社会理论基础
- **Durkheim (1898)**: Collective consciousness explanation
- **涂尔干 (1898)**: 集体意识解释
- **Weber (1922)**: Individual intentional attitudes with shared aims
- **韦伯 (1922)**: 具有共享目标的个体意向态度
- **Collingwood (1947)**: Society as joint will - "We will go for this walk"
- **柯林伍德 (1947)**: 社会作为联合意志 - "我们将去散步"
