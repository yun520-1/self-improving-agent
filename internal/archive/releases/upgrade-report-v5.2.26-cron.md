## Theoretical Additions Summary | 理论新增摘要

### 1. Emotion Prototype Structure | 情绪原型结构

**Key Insight | 核心洞见**: Emotions are categorized by similarity to prototypes, not by necessary and sufficient conditions.

**Implementation | 实现**:
- Multi-dimensional typicality scoring (5 dimensions)
- Borderline case identification (boredom, curiosity, surprise)
- Exemplar-based recognition alongside prototype matching
- Perceptual symbol integration for embodied grounding

**Impact | 影响**: Better emotion category boundary detection + improved atypical instance recognition

### 2. First-Person Authority | 第一人称权威

**Key Insight | 核心洞见**: Some self-ascriptions of emotions cannot be wrong about who is feeling (Immunity to Error Through Misidentification).

**Implementation | 实现**:
- IEM judgment classification (interoceptive/observational/testimonial)
- Confidence calibration based on IEM status
- Integration with phenomenological self-consciousness module

**Impact | 影响**: More accurate emotion attribution confidence + better self-knowledge modeling

### 3. Trust-Based Collective Intentionality | 信任基础的集体意向性

**Key Insight | 核心洞见**: Trust (cognitive + normative components) is the attitude underlying collective intentionality.

**Implementation | 实现**:
- Schmid (2013) trust model integration
- Walther-Scheler synthesis (four-level shared experience + irreducibly collective emotions)
- Trust assessment in collective emotion detection

**Impact | 影响**: Better shared experience quality evaluation + improved collective emotion detection

### 4. Aesthetic Emotion Framework | 审美情绪框架

**Key Insight | 核心洞见**: Aesthetic emotions (beauty, interest, curiosity, sublime, nostalgia, awe) have distinct appraisal patterns and action tendencies.

**Implementation | 实现**:
- Six aesthetic emotion types with full appraisal profiles
- Integration with narrative psychology (nuclear episodes)
- Cultivation interventions for each emotion type

**Impact | 影响**: Expanded emotion coverage + better art/music/nature experience support
