## Key Insights | 关键洞察

### Theoretical Breakthroughs | 理论突破

1. **Pre-reflective Self as Foundation**  
   **前反思自我作为基础**  
   The minimal self (pre-reflective self-consciousness) provides the grounding for all emotional experience. This bridges phenomenology with enactivism.  
   最小自我（前反思自我意识）为所有情绪体验提供基础。这连接了现象学与生成主义。

2. **Narrative as Self-Production**  
   **叙事作为自我生产**  
   Life stories are not just descriptions—they are constitutive of identity. This aligns narrative psychology with autopoiesis theory.  
   生命故事不仅是描述——它们构成身份。这使叙事心理学与自创生理论一致。

3. **Temporal Structure of Emotion**  
   **情绪的时间结构**  
   Emotions unfold across retention-primal impression-protention. This integrates Husserlian time-consciousness with predictive processing.  
   情绪在保持 - 原印象 - 预期中展开。这整合了胡塞尔时间意识与预测加工。
