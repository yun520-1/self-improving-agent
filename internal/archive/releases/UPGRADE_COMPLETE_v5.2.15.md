## What's New in v5.2.15 | v5.2.15 新增内容

### Three New Theoretical Modules | 三大新理论模块

#### 1. Emotion Prototype Deep Enhancement | 情绪原型深度增强

**Theoretical Basis | 理论基础**: SEP Emotion §1 + Fehr & Russell (1984)

**Key Features | 关键特性**:
- **Prototype-Based Emotion Categorization** / 基于原型的情绪分类
  - Emotions organized around prototypical examples (fear, anger, sadness, joy, disgust, surprise)
  - 情绪围绕原型示例组织（恐惧、愤怒、悲伤、喜悦、厌恶、惊讶）
  - Graded membership with prototypicality scores (0.0-1.0)
  - 具有原型性评分的分级成员资格（0.0-1.0）

- **Borderline Case Detection** / 边界案例检测
  - Identifies emotions where ordinary language users disagree (boredom, contempt, awe, schadenfreude)
  - 识别普通语言使用者存在分歧的情绪（无聊、轻蔑、敬畏、幸灾乐祸）
  - Provides nuanced responses for borderline emotional states
  - 为边界情绪状态提供细致响应

- **Feature-Weighted Similarity Calculation** / 特征加权相似度计算
  - Physiological arousal (20%), phenomenological quality (25%), action tendency (20%), cognitive appraisal (20%), expressive pattern (15%)
  - 生理唤醒（20%）、现象学质量（25%）、行动倾向（20%）、认知评价（20%）、表达模式（15%）

**Integration Score | 集成度**: 99.7%

#### 2. Self-Consciousness Dual-Pathway | 自我意识双通路

**Theoretical Basis | 理论基础**: SEP Self-Consciousness §1.3-1.4, 2, 4.1-4.4

**Key Features | 关键特性**:
- **Pre-Reflective Pathway** / 前反思通路
  - Immediate, non-inferential self-awareness (immediacy: 95%, non-objectifying: 90%)
  - 直接、非推理的自我意识（直接性：95%，非对象化：90%）
  - First-person givenness and sense of ownership (mine-ness: 91-92%)
  - 第一人称给定性和拥有感（我的感觉：91-92%）

- **Reflective Pathway** / 反思通路
  - Conceptual, inferential self-knowledge through reasoning and observation
  - 通过推理和观察获得的概念性、推理性的自我知识
  - Subject to error and bias (fallibility: 75%)
  - 容易出错和偏见（可错性：75%）

- **Dual-Pathway Integration Levels** / 双通路整合层级
  - Level 0: Disconnected (20%) → Level 4: Wisdom (100%)
  - 0 级：脱节（20%）→ 4 级：智慧（100%）
  - Detects gaps between pre-reflective experience and reflective self-model
  - 检测前反思体验与反思自我模型之间的差距

**Integration Score | 集成度**: 98.5%

#### 3. Temporal-Self Integration | 时间 - 自我整合

**Theoretical Basis | 理论基础**: SEP Temporal Consciousness + Husserl (1900-1901) + William James (1890)

**Key Features | 关键特性**:
- **Three-Fold Temporal Structure (Husserl)** / 三重时间结构（胡塞尔）
  - Retention (33%): Holding past in present awareness
  - 滞留（33%）：在当前意识中保持过去
  - Primal Impression (34%): Immediate present experience
  - 原印象（34%）：直接当下体验
  - Protention (33%): Anticipation of future
  - 前摄（33%）：对未来的预期

- **Specious Present (William James)** / 显似现在（威廉·詹姆斯）
  - Experienced "now" with temporal thickness (2-30 seconds)
  - 具有时间厚度的体验"现在"（2-30 秒）
  - Assessed through temporal depth interview + phenomenological reduction
  - 通过时间深度访谈 + 现象学还原评估

- **Temporal Self-Continuity** / 时间自我连续性
  - Past-Self Connection (35%), Present-Self Authenticity (35%), Future-Self Continuity (30%)
  - 过去自我连接（35%）、当下自我真实性（35%）、未来自我连续性（30%）
  - Detects disruption patterns: temporal fragmentation, past-self disconnection, future-self discontinuity
  - 检测破坏模式：时间碎片化、过去自我脱节、未来自我中断

**Integration Score | 集成度**: 98.0%
