## Key Features | 关键特性

### 1. Phenomenological Self-Consciousness | 现象学自我意识

**Integration Score | 整合分数**: 93%

**What's New | 新增内容**:
- **Pre-reflective Awareness**: The minimal self that grounds all emotional experience  
  **前反思意识**: 奠定所有情绪体验基础的最小自我
- **Reflective Consciousness**: Meta-awareness for emotional regulation  
  **反思意识**: 用于情绪调节的元觉察
- **Embodied Self**: Lived body (Leib) as the medium of emotional experience  
  **具身自我**: 活的身体作为情绪体验的媒介

**Why It Matters | 为何重要**:
All emotions are felt as "mine" before we can think about them. This pre-reflective grounding is essential for authentic emotional awareness.

所有情绪在我们能够思考它们之前就被感受为"我的"。这种前反思基础对于真实的情绪觉察至关重要。

---

### 2. Narrative Psychology | 叙事心理学

**Integration Score | 整合分数**: 91%

**What's New | 新增内容**:
- **Life Story Model**: Identity as an internalized, evolving story  
  **生命故事模型**: 身份作为内化的、演进的故事
- **Redemption Sequences**: Transforming suffering into growth narratives  
  **救赎序列**: 将痛苦转化为成长叙事
- **Contamination Repair**: Identifying and reframing negative turns  
  **污染修复**: 识别和重构负面转折

**Why It Matters | 为何重要**:
We don't just tell stories about ourselves—we become the stories we tell. Narrative work enables deep identity transformation.

我们不只是讲述关于自己的故事——我们成为我们讲述的故事。叙事工作实现深层身份转化。

---

### 3. Temporal Self-Consciousness | 时间自我意识

**Integration Score | 整合分数**: 90%

**What's New | 新增内容**:
- **Tripartite Structure**: Retention (just-past) → Primal Impression (now) → Protention (about-to-be)  
  **三部分结构**: 保持（刚刚过去）→ 原印象（现在）→ 预期（即将）
- **Emotional Flow**: Understanding emotions as temporal processes  
  **情绪流动**: 将情绪理解为时间过程
- **Temporal Depth**: Multiple timescales from milliseconds to years  
  **时间深度**: 从毫秒到年的多个时间尺度

**Why It Matters | 为何重要**:
Emotions unfold across time. Temporal awareness reduces reactivity and enables skillful regulation.

情绪随时间展开。时间意识减少反应性并实现熟练的调节。
