## What's New | 新增内容

### 🧠 Core Theoretical Integration | 焦点理论整合

#### 1. Enactivism (生成主义) - 92% Integrated

**Key Insight | 核心洞见**: Cognition is not representation but embodied action that brings forth a meaningful world.  
认知不是表征而是带出有意义世界的具身行动。

**Four E's of Cognition | 认知的四个 E**:
- **Embodied | 具身的**: Cognition depends on bodily experience
- **Embedded | 嵌入的**: Cognition occurs in real environments
- **Enactive | 生成的**: Cognition brings forth world through action
- **Extended | 延展的**: Cognition extends into body and environment

**Application | 应用**: Emotion as world-disclosing + meaning-generating + action-oriented  
情绪作为世界揭示 + 意义生成 + 行动导向

---

#### 2. Autopoiesis Theory (自创生理论) - 90% Integrated

**Key Insight | 核心洞见**: Living systems are self-producing networks that maintain organizational closure.  
生命系统是维持组织闭合的自我生产网络。

**Core Concepts | 核心概念**:
- **Organizational Closure | 组织闭合**: Self-producing network of processes
- **Structural Coupling | 结构耦合**: History of recurrent interactions
- **Identity | 身份**: Self-produced unity distinguishing system from environment

**Application | 应用**: Emotional identity as self-constituting  
情绪身份作为自我构成

---

#### 3. Adaptivity and Normativity (适应性与规范性) - 89% Integrated

**Key Insight | 核心洞见**: Living systems establish their own norms through self-production.  
生命系统通过自我生产建立自己的规范。

**Core Mechanisms | 核心机制**:
- **Normativity | 规范性**: Viability boundaries define good/bad
- **Adaptivity | 适应性**: Capacity to modulate coupling with environment
- **Valence | 效价**: Signals adaptive significance

**Application | 应用**: Emotional adaptivity training  
情绪适应性训练

---

#### 4. Participatory Sense-Making (参与式意义创造) - 91% Integrated

**Key Insight | 核心洞见**: Social understanding is participatory sense-making through interactive coordination.  
社会理解是通过互动协调的参与式意义创造。

**Interactive Coordination | 互动协调**:
- **Rhythmic Coupling | 节奏耦合**: Milliseconds scale
- **Affective Attunement | 情感调谐**: Seconds scale
- **Intentional Alignment | 意向对齐**: Minutes scale
- **Narrative Co-construction | 叙事共同建构**: Hours scale

**Application | 应用**: Therapeutic alliance as participatory sense-making  
治疗联盟作为参与式意义创造
