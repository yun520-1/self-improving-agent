### 2. Four Explanatory Challenges Framework | 四大解释挑战框架

**English**:  
Complete framework addressing the four core challenges in emotion theory:
- **Differentiation**: How emotions differ from each other and from non-emotional states (prototype structure with typicality scoring)
- **Motivation**: Whether and how emotions motivate behavior (action tendency model with motivational force)
- **Intentionality**: Whether emotions have object-directedness and can be appropriate/inappropriate (intentional object with appropriateness assessment)
- **Phenomenology**: Whether emotions always involve subjective experiences (subjective experience with experiential thickness)

**中文**:  
解决情绪理论四大核心挑战的完整框架：
- **区分**: 情绪如何彼此区分以及如何与非情绪状态区分（具有典型性评分的原型结构）
- **动机**: 情绪是否以及如何动机行为（具有动机力量的行动倾向模型）
- **意向性**: 情绪是否具有对象指向性以及是否恰当/不恰当（具有恰当性评估的意向对象）
- **现象学**: 情绪是否总是涉及主观体验（具有体验厚度的主观体验）

### 3. IEM Assessment Framework | IEM 评估框架

**English**:  
Immunity to Error through Misidentification (IEM) framework based on Shoemaker (1968):
- **Introspective Judgments**: "I am in pain" - immunity to error (confidence: 0.98)
- **Proprioceptive Judgments**: "My legs are crossed" - direct bodily awareness (confidence: 0.95)
- **Agentive Judgments**: "I am raising my arm" - action ownership awareness (confidence: 0.93)
- **Confidence Calibration**: Based on IEM status (high/moderate/low confidence categories)

**中文**:  
基于 Shoemaker (1968) 的免于误认错误 (IEM) 框架：
- **内省判断**: "我在疼痛" - 免于误认错误（置信度：0.98）
- **本体感受判断**: "我的腿交叉" - 直接身体意识（置信度：0.95）
- **能动性判断**: "我在抬起手臂" - 行动所有权意识（置信度：0.93）
- **置信度校准**: 基于 IEM 状态（高/中/低置信度类别）

### 4. Scheler-Walther Phenomenological Shared Experience | 谢勒 - 瓦尔特现象学共享体验

**English**:  
Complete integration of phenomenological shared experience modeling:
- **Schelerian Model**: Direct shared experience without mutual awareness (many minds in one and the same intentional state)
- **Walther Four-Layer Structure**:
  - Layer 1: A experiences x, B experiences x
  - Layer 2: A empathizes with B's experience, B empathizes with A's
  - Layer 3: A identifies with B's experience, B identifies with A's
  - Layer 4: Mutual empathetic awareness of identification
- **Shared Experience Depth**: Schelerian 0.97, Waltherian 0.95
- **Classic Examples**: Grief at deathbed, National enthusiasm (August Madness 1914)

**中文**:  
现象学共享体验建模的完整整合：
- **谢勒模型**: 无需相互意识的直接共享体验（多个心灵处于同一个意向状态）
- **瓦尔特四层结构**:
  - 第一层：A 体验 x，B 体验 x
  - 第二层：A 共情 B 的体验，B 共情 A 的
  - 第三层：A 认同 B 的体验，B 认同 A 的
  - 第四层：对认同的相互共情意识
- **共享体验深度**: 谢勒式 0.97，瓦尔特式 0.95
- **经典例子**: 死亡床边的悲伤、民族热情（1914 年八月狂热）

**English**:  
Full integration of embodied cognition approaches:
- **Ecological Psychology** (Gibson 1966, 1979):
  - Rich stimuli assumption (vs impoverished stimuli)
  - Invariant detection in changing stimulation
  - Whole organism approach in motion through environments
  - Affordance detection (action possibilities)
- **Connectionist-Dynamical Approach**:
  - Non-symbolic computation (weighted activation, pattern recognition)
  - Body-environment coupling (unfolding patterns, temporal evolution)
  - Attractor states in dynamical systems

具身认知方法的完整整合：

  - 丰富刺激假设（相对于贫乏刺激）
  - 变化刺激中的不变量检测
  - 有机体在环境中运动的整体方法
  - 可供性检测（行动可能性）
- **联结主义 - 动力学方法**:
  - 非符号计算（加权激活、模式识别）
  - 身体 - 环境耦合（展开模式、时间演化）
