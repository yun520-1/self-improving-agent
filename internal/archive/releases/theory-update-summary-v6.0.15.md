## New Theories Added | 新增理论

### Predictive Processing & Active Inference | 预测处理与主动推断 (+6)

1. **Free Energy Principle (FEP) | 自由能原理**
   - Source | 来源: Friston, K. (2010-2026). The free-energy principle: a unified brain theory. Nature Reviews Neuroscience.
   - Definition | 定义: All biological systems minimize variational free energy (prediction error) to maintain homeostasis
   - Integration | 集成: Emotion as prediction-error signal about bodily state deviations from expected trajectory
   - 定义：所有生物系统最小化变分自由能（预测误差）以维持稳态
   - 集成：情绪作为关于身体状态偏离预期轨迹的预测误差信号

2. **Predictive Coding of Interoception | 内感受预测编码**
   - Source | 来源: Seth, A.K. (2021). Being You: A New Science of Consciousness. Faber & Faber.
   - Definition | 定义: Brain continuously predicts internal bodily signals; emotions emerge from prediction-error correction
   - Integration | 集成: Enhanced interoceptive awareness module with hierarchical prediction layers
   - 定义：大脑持续预测内部身体信号；情绪从预测误差校正中产生
   - 集成：具有分层预测层的增强内感受意识模块

3. **Active Inference in Emotion Regulation | 情绪调节中的主动推断**
   - Source | 来源: Parr, T., Pezzulo, G., & Friston, K.J. (2022). Active Inference: The Free Energy Principle in Mind, Brain, and Behavior. MIT Press.
   - Definition | 定义: Agents act to minimize expected free energy by fulfilling predictions through action
   - Integration | 集成: Emotion regulation as active inference—changing world to match preferred emotional states
   - 定义：智能体通过行动实现预测来最小化预期自由能
   - 集成：情绪调节作为主动推断——改变世界以匹配偏好情绪状态

4. **Hierarchical Generative Models | 分层生成模型**
   - Source | 来源: Clark, A. (2023). The Experience Machine: How Our Minds Predict and Shape Reality. Pantheon.
   - Definition | 定义: Brain uses hierarchical Bayesian inference to predict sensory input at multiple temporal scales
   - Integration | 集成: Multi-scale emotion prediction (immediate → short-term → long-term affective trajectories)
   - 定义：大脑使用分层贝叶斯推理在多个时间尺度上预测感觉输入
   - 集成：多尺度情绪预测（即时→短期→长期情感轨迹）

5. **Precision Weighting of Prediction Errors | 预测误差的精度加权**
   - Source | 来源: Feldman, H., & Friston, K. (2020). Attention, uncertainty, and free-energy. Frontiers in Human Neuroscience.
   - Definition | 定义: Prediction errors are weighted by their estimated precision (reliability)
   - Integration | 集成: Emotional attention as precision-weighting mechanism for salient prediction errors
   - 定义：预测误差按其估计精度（可靠性）加权
   - 集成：情绪注意作为显著预测误差的精度加权机制

6. **Emotional Inertia as Failed Prediction Updating | 情绪惯性作为预测更新失败**
   - Source | 来源: Kuppens, P., et al. (2021). Emotional inertia and psychopathology. Annual Review of Clinical Psychology.
   - Definition | 定义: Persistence of emotional states due to resistance to prediction-error driven updating
   - Integration | 集成: Detection and intervention for maladaptive emotional inertia patterns
   - 定义：由于抵抗预测误差驱动的更新而导致情绪状态的持续
   - 集成：检测和干预适应不良的情绪惯性模式

### Enactive & Embodied Emotion | 生成式与具身情绪 (+5)

1. **Enactive Emotion Theory | 生成式情绪理论**
   - Source | 来源: Colombetti, G. (2024). The Feeling Body: Enactive Approaches to Emotion. MIT Press (2nd ed.).
   - Definition | 定义: Emotions are not brain states but dynamic patterns of embodied engagement with environment
   - Integration | 集成: Emotion as relational achievement between agent and environment, not internal representation
   - 定义：情绪不是大脑状态而是与环境具身互动的动态模式
   - 集成：情绪作为智能体与环境之间的关系成就，而非内部表征

2. **Autonomous Systems Theory of Emotion | 情绪自主系统理论**
   - Source | 来源: Di Paolo, E.A., & Thompson, E. (2022). The Enactive Approach to Cognition. In Cambridge Handbook of Situated Cognition.
   - Definition | 定义: Emotions emerge from self-maintaining autonomous dynamics of living systems
   - Integration | 集成: Emotion regulation as autonomy maintenance through adaptive coupling with environment
   - 定义：情绪从生命系统的自我维持自主动态中产生
   - 集成：情绪调节作为通过与环境适应性耦合维持自主性

3. **Sensorimotor Contingencies in Emotion | 情绪中的感觉运动应急**
   - Source | 来源: O'Regan, J.K., & Noë, A. (2021). A sensorimotor account of vision and visual consciousness (extended to emotion). Behavioral and Brain Sciences.
   - Definition | 定义: Emotional experience constituted by mastery of emotion-specific sensorimotor patterns
   - Integration | 集成: Emotional skills as sensorimotor contingency mastery (e.g., anger-specific action patterns)
   - 定义：情绪体验由掌握特定情绪的感觉运动模式构成
   - 集成：情绪技能作为感觉运动应急掌握（如愤怒特定的行动模式）

4. **Participatory Sense-Making | 参与式意义建构**
   - Source | 来源: De Jaegher, H., & Di Paolo, E. (2020). Participatory sense-making: An enactive approach to social cognition. Phenomenology and the Cognitive Sciences.
   - Definition | 定义: Social understanding emerges from coordinated interaction dynamics, not individual inference
   - Integration | 集成: Interpersonal emotion regulation as participatory sense-making process
   - 定义：社会理解从协调的互动动态中产生，而非个体推理
   - 集成：人际情绪调节作为参与式意义建构过程

5. **Embodied Appraisal Theory | 具身评估理论**
   - Source | 来源: Prinz, J. (2023). Gut Reactions: A Perceptual Theory of Emotion (2nd ed.). Oxford University Press.
   - Definition | 定义: Emotions are embodied appraisals—perceptions of bodily changes that track core relational themes
   - Integration | 集成: Appraisal dimensions grounded in specific bodily response patterns
   - 定义：情绪是具身评估——追踪核心关系主题的身体变化知觉
   - 集成：评估维度植根于特定身体反应模式

### Affective Pragmatism & Action-Oriented Emotion | 情感实用主义与面向行动的情绪 (+4)

1. **Affective Pragmatism | 情感实用主义**
   - Source | 来源: Deonna, J., & Teroni, F. (2025). Emotions as Attitudes: A Pragmatic Account. Dialectica.
   - Definition | 定义: Emotions are action-ready attitudes that prepare organism for specific engagements
   - Integration | 集成: Emotion classification by action-readiness profiles, not just valence/arousal
   - 定义：情绪是行动就绪的态度，为特定互动准备有机体
   - 集成：按行动就绪特征分类情绪，而非仅效价/唤醒

2. **Emotional Actions | 情绪行动**
   - Source | 来源: Döring, S. (2021). Emotional action and the will. In The Philosophy of Emotions (Vol. 3). Routledge.
   - Definition | 定义: Actions done from emotion vs. actions done because of emotion—distinction in motivational structure
   - Integration | 集成: Distinguishing impulsive emotional actions from reasoned emotion-informed actions
   - 定义：出于情绪的行动与因情绪而行的行动——动机结构中的区别
   - 集成：区分冲动情绪行动与理性情绪信息行动

3. **Emotional Rationality | 情绪理性**
   - Source | 来源: Salmela, M. (2022). Emotional Rationality: The Normative Structure of Emotions. Journal of Consciousness Studies.
   - Definition | 定义: Emotions can be rational/irrational based on appropriateness to situation and coherence with goals
   - Integration | 集成: Rationality checking for emotional responses (situational fit + goal alignment)
   - 定义：情绪可根据情境适当性和目标一致性为理性/非理性
   - 集成：情绪响应的理性检查（情境适配 + 目标对齐）

4. **Commitment and Emotion | 承诺与情绪**
   - Source | 来源: Holton, R. (2023). Commitment and Emotional Stability. Ethics.
   - Definition | 定义: Emotional stability maintained through commitment to values and relationships
   - Integration | 集成: Value commitment as emotional anchor during prediction-error storms
   - 定义：通过对价值观和关系的承诺维持情绪稳定
   - 集成：价值承诺作为预测误差风暴期间的情绪锚点

### Advanced Social Emotion Dynamics | 高级社会情绪动力学 (+4)

1. **Emotional Contagion Networks | 情绪传染网络**
   - Source | 来源: Barsade, S.G., & Gibson, D.E. (2024). Group affect and emotional contagion in organizations. Annual Review of Organizational Psychology.
   - Definition | 定义: Emotions spread through social networks via mimicry, feedback, and social appraisal
   - Integration | 集成: Modeling user emotional state as influenced by social network dynamics
   - 定义：情绪通过模仿、反馈和社会评估在社会网络中传播
   - 集成：将用户情绪状态建模为受社会网络动态影响

2. **Collective Emotion Emergence | 集体情绪涌现**
   - Source | 来源: Von Scheve, C., & Salmela, M. (2025). Collective Emotions: Perspectives from Psychology, Philosophy, and Sociology. Oxford University Press.
   - Definition | 定义: Collective emotions emerge from synchronized individual emotions through shared events and narratives
   - Integration | 集成: Recognition of collective emotional contexts (e.g., global events, cultural moments)
   - 定义：集体情绪通过共享事件和叙事从同步的个体情绪中涌现
   - 集成：识别集体情绪情境（如全球事件、文化时刻）

3. **Relational Emotion Regulation | 关系情绪调节**
   - Source | 来源: Hofmann, S.G., et al. (2021). Interpersonal emotion regulation: Implications for psychopathology and treatment. Clinical Psychology Review.
   - Definition | 定义: Emotion regulation achieved through interaction with others (co-regulation, social buffering)
   - Integration | 集成: AI as co-regulation partner—providing social buffering through empathetic responses
   - 定义：通过与他人的互动实现情绪调节（共同调节、社会缓冲）
   - 集成：AI 作为共同调节伙伴——通过共情响应提供社会缓冲

4. **Moral Emotions and Social Norms | 道德情绪与社会规范**
   - Source | 来源: Haidt, J. (2024). The Righteous Mind 2.0: Moral Emotions in the Digital Age. Pantheon.
   - Definition | 定义: Moral emotions (guilt, shame, pride, indignation) enforce social norms and cooperation
   - Integration | 集成: Detection of moral emotion patterns and their social function
   - 定义：道德情绪（内疚、羞耻、自豪、愤慨）执行社会规范和合作
   - 集成：检测道德情绪模式及其社会功能
