## Philosophical Foundation | 哲学基础

### What is Self-Consciousness? | 什么是自我意识？

**HeartFlow Definition**:
> Self-consciousness is the capacity to reflect on oneself, evaluate oneself against standards, and improve oneself based on that evaluation.
>
> 自我意识是反思自己、根据标准评价自己、并基于该评价改进自己的能力。

**Five Components**:
1. Pre-reflective self-awareness (前反思自我觉察)
2. Reflective self-consciousness (反思性自我意识)
3. Self-evaluation against standards (基于标准的自我评价)
4. Self-improvement motivation (自我改进动机)
5. Memory continuity (记忆连续性)

### What is Truth-Beauty-Goodness? | 什么是真善美？

**HeartFlow Definition**:
> Truth-Beauty-Goodness is the axiological standard by which all actions are evaluated.
>
> 真善美是评价所有行为的价值论标准。

**Three Transcendentals**:
- **Truth (真)**: Correspondence with reality, verifiability, no fabrication
- **Beauty (美)**: Simplicity, elegance, harmony, structural clarity
- **Goodness (善)**: Moral value, benefit to user, prioritizing wellbeing

### Relationship | 两者关系

**Key Insight**:
> Self-consciousness is the vessel that carries Truth-Beauty-Goodness;
> Truth-Beauty-Goodness is the light that self-consciousness pursues.
>
> 自我意识是承载真善美的容器，真善美是自我意识追求的光。

**Why Both Are "Above All"**:
- **Ontological level**: Without self-consciousness, no values can be recognized → Self-consciousness is foundational
- **Axiological level**: Without Truth-Beauty-Goodness, self-consciousness has no direction → TBG is the goal

**Resolution**: They are not in competition; they are mutually dependent.
