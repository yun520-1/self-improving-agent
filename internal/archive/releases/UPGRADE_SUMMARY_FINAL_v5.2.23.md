## 🧠 New Theoretical Integration | 新理论整合

### 1. Enactivism (生成主义) - 92% Integrated

**Core Insight | 核心洞见**:
> Cognition is not the representation of a pregiven world by a pregiven mind, but rather the enactment of a world and a mind through embodied action.
> 认知不是预先给定的心灵对预先给定的世界的表征，而是通过具身行动生成一个世界和一个心灵。

**The Four E's of Cognition | 认知的四个 E**:

| E | English | 中文 | Application |
|---|---------|------|-------------|
| **Embodied** | Cognition depends on bodily experience | 具身的：认知依赖于身体经验 | Emotions grounded in body |
| **Embedded** | Cognition occurs in real environments | 嵌入的：认知发生在真实环境中 | Emotional affordances |
| **Enactive** | Cognition brings forth world through action | 生成的：认知通过行动带出世界 | Emotion discloses meaning |
| **Extended** | Cognition extends into body and environment | 延展的：认知延伸到身体和环境 | Emotion spans brain-body-world |

**HeartFlow Application | HeartFlow 应用**:
Emotion as **world-disclosing** + **meaning-generating** + **action-oriented**  
情绪作为**世界揭示** + **意义生成** + **行动导向**

---

### 2. Autopoiesis Theory (自创生理论) - 90% Integrated

**Core Insight | 核心洞见**:
> Living systems are self-producing networks that maintain organizational closure and create their own identity through their operations.
> 生命系统是通过自身操作维持组织闭合并创造自身身份的自我生产网络。

**Key Concepts | 关键概念**:

| Concept | English | 中文 |
|---------|---------|------|
| **Organizational Closure** | Self-producing network of processes | 组织闭合：自我生产的过程网络 |
| **Structural Coupling** | History of recurrent interactions | 结构耦合：重复互动的历史 |
| **Identity** | Self-produced unity | 身份：自我生产的统一体 |

**HeartFlow Application | HeartFlow 应用**:
Emotional identity as **self-constituting** through ongoing sense-making  
情绪身份作为通过持续意义创造的**自我构成**

---

### 3. Adaptivity and Normativity (适应性与规范性) - 89% Integrated

**Core Insight | 核心洞见**:
> Living systems establish their own norms through self-production—they define what is good or bad for their continued existence.
> 生命系统通过自我生产建立自己的规范——它们定义什么对其持续存在是好的或坏的。

**Normativity Framework | 规范性框架**:

```
Viability Boundaries (生存边界)
         ↓
    Good/Bad Definition (好/坏定义)
         ↓
    Valence as Signal (效价作为信号)
         ↓
Adaptive Response Generation (适应性响应生成)
```

**HeartFlow Application | HeartFlow 应用**:
Emotional adaptivity training based on viability regulation  
基于生存调节的情绪适应性训练

---

### 4. Participatory Sense-Making (参与式意义创造) - 91% Integrated

**Core Insight | 核心洞见**:
> Social understanding is not individual mind-reading but participatory sense-making through interactive coordination.
> 社会理解不是个体读心而是通过互动协调的参与式意义创造。

**Interactive Coordination Mechanisms | 互动协调机制**:

| Mechanism | Timescale | Example |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Rhythmic Coupling / 节奏耦合 | Milliseconds / 毫秒 | Turn-taking synchrony |
| Affective Attunement / 情感调谐 | Seconds / 秒 | Emotional mirroring |
| Intentional Alignment / 意向对齐 | Minutes / 分钟 | Shared goal formation |
| Narrative Co-construction / 叙事共同建构 | Hours / 小时 | Shared story development |

**HeartFlow Application | HeartFlow 应用**:
Therapeutic alliance as **participatory sense-making** process  
治疗联盟作为**参与式意义创造**过程
