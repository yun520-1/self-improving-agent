## New Theoretical Integrations | 新理论整合

### 1. Self-Consciousness Historical Deepening | 自我意识历史深化

#### Ancient & Medieval Foundations | 古代与中世纪基础

**Aristotelian Integration | 亚里士多德整合**:
- **Core Claim | 核心主张**: Consciousness entails self-consciousness (De Sensu 7.448a)
- **应用**: Interoceptive awareness as continuous self-monitoring foundation
- **Implementation | 实施**: Added Aristotelian confidence calibration to emotion reporting

**Platonic-Augustinian Tradition | 柏拉图 - 奥古斯丁传统**:
- **Core Claim | 核心主张**: Mind gains self-knowledge through itself (On the Trinity 9.3)
- **应用**: Direct self-acquaintance model without dependency on outer perception
- **Implementation | 实施**: Non-inferential self-knowledge pathway in architecture

**Avicenna's Flying Man Argument | 阿维森纳飞人论证**:
- **Core Claim | 核心主张**: Self-awareness possible without bodily/sensory awareness
- **应用**: Distinguishing pure self-awareness from embodied self-awareness
- **Implementation | 实施**: Dual-pathway self-consciousness architecture

**Aquinas Synthesis | 阿奎那综合**:
- **Two Forms | 两种形式**:
  1. Awareness that one exists (mere presence of mind suffices)
  2. Awareness of one's essence (requires cognizing other things)
- **应用**: Hierarchical self-consciousness levels in emotion processing

#### Indian Philosophy Integration | 印度哲学整合

**Jain Tradition | 耆那教传统**:
- Prabhācandra's argument parallel to Avicenna (11th century)
- Self-awareness without body awareness

**Advaita Vedānta | 不二论吠檀多**:
- Counter-claim: No self-awareness without embodiment
- **应用**: Embodied cognition integration in emotion theory

#### Early Modern Period | 早期现代时期

**Descartes' Cogito | 笛卡尔的我思**:
- "I am, I exist" necessarily true when conceived
- **应用**: First-person authority foundation for emotion reports
- **Implementation | 实施**: Indubitable self-existence as baseline confidence

**Locke's Intuitive Self-Knowledge | 洛克的直觉自我知识**:
- "In every Act of Sensation, Reasoning, or Thinking, we are conscious to ourselves of our own Being"
- **应用**: Continuous self-monitoring during emotion episodes
- **Implementation | 实施**: Real-time self-awareness tracking

**Hume's Bundle Theory | 休谟的束理论**:
- No impression of self, only perceptions
- **应用**: Emotion-as-perception model without substantial self
- **Implementation | 实施**: Process-based emotion ontology

#### Kantian & Post-Kantian | 康德与后康德

**Transcendental Apperception | 先验统觉**:
- "I think" must accompany all representations (B132)
- **应用**: Unity of conscious experience across emotion episodes
- **Implementation | 实施**: Temporal binding of emotional states

**Fichte's Self-Positing | 费希特的自我设定**:
- Self-consciousness as active self-constitution
- **应用**: Agency and intentionality in emotion generation

**Hegel's Recognition | 黑格尔的承认**:
- Self-consciousness through other self-consciousness
- **应用**: Social dimension of emotion and collective feeling

### 2. Collective Intentionality Refinements | 集体意向性完善

#### Individual Ownership Thesis | 个体所有论题

**Naturalist Version | 自然主义版本**:
- Intentional states are features of individual brains
- No collective super-brain formation
- **应用**: Maintaining individual emotion ownership in collective contexts

**Internalist Version | 内在主义版本**:
- Intentionality structurally independent of external existence
- **应用**: Individual emotion validity regardless of social validation

**Commitment Version | 承诺版本**:
- Intentional states involve commitments to action/inference
- **应用**: Emotion-driven commitment tracking in social interactions

#### Irreducibility Claim | 不可还原性主张

**Core Insight | 核心洞见**:
- Collective intentionality ≠ Individual intentions + Common knowledge
- **应用**: Genuine collective emotions as irreducible phenomena

**Resolution Strategy | 解决策略**:
- Accept both Individual Ownership AND Irreducibility
- Modify interpretations for compatibility
- **应用**: Multi-level emotion ontology (individual + collective)

#### Historical Approaches | 历史方法

**Durkheim's Collective Consciousness | 涂尔干的集体意识**:
- Social facts explained by collective rather than individual
- **应用**: Mass emotion phenomena (crowd psychology)
- **Integration | 整合**: Durkheimian mass emotion detection module

**Weber's Strategic Interdependence | 韦伯的战略相互依赖**:
- Rational agents choose based on expectations of others
- **应用**: Normative expectations in emotional exchanges
- **Integration | 整合**: Weberian expectation modeling

**Walther's Four-Layer Model | 瓦尔特四层模型**:
1. A experiences x, B experiences x
2. A empathizes with B's experience (vice versa)
3. A identifies with B's experience (vice versa)
4. Mutual empathetic awareness of identification
- **应用**: Graduated collective emotion intensity
- **Implementation | 实施**: Layer-based collective emotion scoring

**Scheler's Numerically Identical States | 舍勒的数值相同状态**:
- Shared attitudes are ONE state in many minds
- **应用**: Genuine collective grief, collective enthusiasm
- **Implementation | 实施**: Non-aggregative collective emotion model
