#### 3. Collective Intentionality Structure | 集体意向性结构

**Core Problem | 核心问题**:
- Individual Ownership Thesis: Each person has their own mind/brain
- 个体所有论题：每个人有自己的心灵/大脑
- Irreducibility Claim: Collective intentionality not reducible to individual + common knowledge
- 不可还原性主张：集体意向性不可还原为个体 + 共同知识

**Resolution | 解决方案**:

| Approach | 方法 | Key Insight | 核心洞见 | Integration | 整合 |
|----------|------|-------------|----------|-------------|------|
| Mode Account | 模式账户 | We-mode vs. I-mode distinction (Tuomela 2007) | 我们模式与我模式区分 | ✅ Complete | 完成 |
| Phenomenological | 现象学 | Numerically identical shared states (Scheler 1954) | 数值相同的共享状态 | ✅ Complete | 完成 |
| Trust-Based | 基于信任 | Cognitive + normative dual components (Schmid 2013) | 认知 + 规范双重成分 | ✅ Complete | 完成 |
| Social Theory | 社会理论 | Collective consciousness vs. individual aims | 集体意识 vs. 个体目标 | ✅ Complete | 完成 |

**Walther's Four-Layer Model | 瓦尔特四层模型**:
1. **Layer 1 | 层 1**: A experiences x, B experiences x / A 体验 x，B 体验 x
2. **Layer 2 | 层 2**: A empathizes with B's experience, vice versa / A 共情 B 的体验，反之亦然
3. **Layer 3 | 层 3**: A identifies with B's experience, vice versa / A 认同 B 的体验，反之亦然
4. **Layer 4 | 层 4**: Mutual empathetic awareness of identification / 对认同的相互共情意识
