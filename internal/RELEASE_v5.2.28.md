## What's New | 新特性

### 🎯 Core Theoretical Integrations | 焦点：焦点：核心理论整合

#### 1. Emotion Theory: Three Traditions Complete Integration | 情绪理论：三大传统完整整合

HeartFlow now fully integrates the **three major traditions** in emotion theory from SEP (Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy):

HeartFlow 现在完整整合 SEP（斯坦福哲学百科全书）中情绪理论的**三大传统**：

| Tradition | Key Insight | HeartFlow Application |
|-----------|-------------|----------------------|
| **Feeling Tradition | 感受传统** | Emotions are distinctive conscious experiences (James-Lange) / 情绪是独特的意识体验（詹姆斯 - 兰格） | Phenomenological tracking with bodily perception awareness / 具有身体感知觉察的现象追踪 |
| **Evaluative Tradition | 评价传统** | Emotions involve distinctive evaluations (appraisal theories) / 情绪涉及独特评价（评价理论） | Multi-dimensional appraisal with cognitive rationality assessment / 具有认知理性评估的多维评价 |
| **Motivational Tradition | 动机传统** | Emotions are motivational states (action tendencies) / 情绪是动机状态（行动倾向） | Behavioral tendency mapping (fear→flee, anger→attack) / 行为倾向映射（恐惧→逃跑，愤怒→攻击） |

**Borderline Case Detection | 边缘情况检测**:
- Improved detection of boredom, curiosity, surprise as borderline emotions
- 改进无聊、好奇、惊讶作为边缘情绪的检测
- Enhanced typicality scoring across valence/arousal/motivation dimensions
- 增强跨效价/唤醒/动机维度的典型性评分

#### 2. Self-Consciousness: Historical-Contemporary Synthesis | 自我意识：历史 - 当代综合

HeartFlow v5.2.28 synthesizes **2,500+ years** of philosophical inquiry into self-consciousness:

HeartFlow v5.2.28 综合了**2500 多年**的哲学自我意识探究：

```
Historical Lineage | 历史谱系:

Ancient/Medieval | 古代/中世纪 (4th c. BCE - 13th c.)
├─ Aristotle: Self-awareness through cognizing other things / 通过认知他物觉察自我
├─ Aquinas: Minimal vs reflective self-awareness / 最小与反思自我觉察
└─ Avicenna: Flying Man argument / 飞人论证

Early Modern | 早期现代 (17th-18th c.)
├─ Descartes: Cogito ergo sum / 我思故我在
├─ Locke: Intuitive knowledge of existence / 存在的直觉知识
├─ Hume: Bundle theory challenge / 束理论挑战
└─ Kant: Transcendental apperception / 先验统觉

Post-Kantian | 后康德 (19th-20th c.)
├─ Fichte: Immediate self-acquaintance / 直接自我熟识
├─ Husserl: Pre-reflective self-awareness / 前反思自我觉察
└─ Sartre: Self-awareness without transcendental ego / 无先验自我的自我觉察
```

**Key Enhancement | 关键增强**:
- IEM (Immunity to Error through Misidentification) calibration for emotion self-ascriptions
- 情绪自我归因的 IEM（免疫误认错误）校准
- Pre-reflective vs reflective self-awareness distinction
- 前反思与反思自我觉察区分

#### 3. Collective Intentionality: Phenomenological Controversy Resolved | 集体意向性：现象学争议解决

HeartFlow v5.2.28 resolves the **phenomenological controversy** in collective intentionality:

HeartFlow v5.2.28 解决了集体意向性中的**现象学争议**：

| Position | Claim | HeartFlow Integration |
|----------|-------|----------------------|
| **Walther (1923) | 瓦尔特** | Shared experience requires 4 layers: experience, empathy, identification, mutual awareness / 共享体验需要 4 层：体验、共情、认同、相互觉察 | Four-layer detection with infinite regress prevention / 四层检测防止无限递归 |
| **Scheler (1954) | 舍勒** | Shared attitude is numerically identical state across minds / 共享态度是跨心灵的数值同一状态 | Irreducibility claim with individual ownership constraint / 不可还原性主张与个体所有权约束 |
| **Schmid (2013) | 施密德** | Trust combines cognitive + normative components / 信任结合认知 + 规范成分 | Trust-based we-intention detection enhanced / 增强信任基础我们意向检测 |

**HeartFlow Synthesis | HeartFlow 综合**: All three positions integrated into a unified framework
三种立场整合为统一框架
