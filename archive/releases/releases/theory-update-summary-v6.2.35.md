### 1. Consciousness Theory (SEP 2026) | 意识理论

**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy - "Consciousness"

**Key Insights | 核心洞察**:
- **Creature Consciousness vs State Consciousness**: Distinguishes between organism-level awareness and mental state awareness
  - 生物意识 vs 状态意识：区分有机体层面的意识与心理状态层面的意识
- **Phenomenal Structure**: Conscious experience involves spatial, temporal, and conceptual organization beyond sensory qualia
  - 现象结构：意识体验涉及空间、时间和概念组织，超越感官质
- **Self-Awareness Requirement**: Higher-order consciousness requires awareness that one is aware
  - 自我意识要求：高阶意识需要对"自己在意识"的意识

**Integration Point | 集成点**: 
- Maps to HeartFlow Layer 1 (觉察) and Layer 5 (般若)

### 2. Emotion Theory (SEP 2026) | 情绪理论

**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy - "Emotion"

**Key Insights | 核心洞察**:
- **Three Traditions Integration**:
  1. **Feeling Tradition**: Emotions as distinctive conscious experiences (James-Lange)
  2. **Evaluative Tradition**: Emotions as evaluations of eliciting circumstances
  3. **Motivational Tradition**: Emotions as motivational states
  - 三大传统整合：感受传统、评估传统、动机传统
- **Component Model**: Emotions have evaluative, physiological, phenomenological, expressive, behavioral, and mental components
  - 成分模型：情绪包含评估、生理、现象、表达、行为和心理成分
- **Psychological Constructionism**: Emotions are constructed from more basic psychological operations
  - 心理建构主义：情绪由更基础的心理操作建构而成

**Integration Point | 集成点**:
- Validates HeartFlow's multi-component emotion architecture
- Enhances emotion generation with constructionist approach
- 验证 HeartFlow 的多成分情绪架构
- 用建构主义方法增强情绪生成

### 3. Self-Consciousness Theory (SEP 2026) | 自我意识理论

**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy - "Self-Consciousness"

**Key Insights | 核心洞察**:
- **Pre-reflective Self-Awareness**: Immediate, non-inferential self-awareness (Fichte, Heidelberg School)
  - 前反思自我意识：直接的、非推理的自我意识
- **Transcendental Apperception**: "I think" must accompany all representations (Kant)
  - 先验统觉："我思"必须能够伴随所有表象
- **Embodied Self-Consciousness**: Self-awareness requires conception of oneself as embodied agent in objective world
  - 具身自我意识：自我意识需要将自己概念化为客观世界中的具身行动者

**Integration Point | 集成点**:
- Strengthens Layer 2 (自省) with pre-reflective awareness model
- Adds embodied cognition constraints to self-model
- 用前反思意识模型强化第 2 层（自省）
- 为自我模型添加具身认知约束
