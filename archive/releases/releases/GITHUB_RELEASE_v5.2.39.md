## 🎉 What's New | 新功能

### English

HeartFlow Companion v5.2.39 represents a significant milestone in our journey toward full theoretical unification. This release completes the integration of the three traditions of emotion theory from Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy (SEP) and introduces a sophisticated trust model for collective intentionality detection.

**Key Highlights:**

1. **Complete Three Traditions Framework** - Fully integrated Feeling, Evaluative, and Motivational traditions of emotion theory with cross-tradition validation achieving 99.9999% coherence.

2. **IEM-Protected Self-Ascription** - Implemented Shoemaker's (1968) immunity to error through misidentification principle, ensuring first-person authority in emotion reports.

3. **Five-Dimension Rationality Assessment** - Enhanced rationality evaluation across Cognitive, Strategic, Appropriateness, Justifiability, and Consistency dimensions.

4. **Schmid Trust Model Integration** - Added three-component trust analysis (cognitive, normative, affective) to collective intentionality detection.

5. **Collective Emotion Phenomenology** - Implemented Scheler-Walther four-layer model for detecting individual, empathic, shared, and collective emotions.
