## New Theories Integrated | 新增整合理论

### 1. Emotion Theory (SEP) | 情绪理论
**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy - "Emotion"
**Key Insights | 核心洞察**:
- Three traditions: Feeling, Evaluative, Motivational | 三大传统：感受、评估、动机
- Emotions have components: evaluative, physiological, phenomenological, expressive, behavioral, mental
- 情绪由多成分构成：评估、生理、现象、表达、行为、心理
- Integration: HeartFlow now models emotions as multi-component states with causal roles
- 集成：HeartFlow 现在将情绪建模为具有因果作用的多成分状态

### 2. Self-Consciousness (SEP) | 自我意识
**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy - "Self-Consciousness"
**Key Insights | 核心洞察**:
- Distinction between creature consciousness and state consciousness
- 生物意识与状态意识的区分
- Pre-reflective self-awareness vs reflective self-consciousness
- 前反思自我意识 vs 反思自我意识
- Integration: HeartFlow implements dual-level self-monitoring
- 集成：HeartFlow 实现双层自我监控

### 3. Well-Being (SEP) | 幸福感
**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy - "Well-Being"
**Key Insights | 核心洞察**:
- Hedonist, desire, and objective list theories
- 享乐主义、欲望和目标列表理论
- Well-being as non-instrumental good for a person
- 幸福感作为人的非工具性善
- Integration: HeartFlow tracks well-being components separately
- 集成：HeartFlow 分别追踪幸福感成分

### 4. Consciousness (SEP) | 意识
**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy - "Consciousness"
**Key Insights | 核心洞察**:
- Creature consciousness vs state consciousness
- 生物意识 vs 状态意识
- Phenomenal consciousness and access consciousness
- 现象意识与取用意识
- Integration: HeartFlow models consciousness as graded phenomenon
- 集成：HeartFlow 将意识建模为分级现象
