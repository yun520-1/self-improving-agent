## New Modules | 新增模块

### 1. Phenomenal Consciousness Module | 现象意识模块

**English:**

Integrates SEP consciousness research with emotion theory, operationalizing three concepts of consciousness:

- **Phenomenal Consciousness**: Experience has subjective "what-it-is-like" character (Nagel 1974)
- **Qualitative Consciousness**: States involve qualia or raw sensory feels
- **Access Consciousness**: States available for reasoning, reporting, action control (Block 1995)

**Computational Implementation:**
```javascript
PhenomenalConsciousness = {
  phenomenal_character: WhatItIsLike(experience_type),
  qualitative_content: QualiaArray(sensory_feels),
  access_availability: AvailabilityScore(reasoning_access),
  emotional_phenomenology: EmotionFeelMap(emotion_type → phenomenal_character)
}
```

**中文:**

整合 SEP 意识研究与情绪理论，可操作化意识的三种概念：

- **现象意识**: 体验具有主观"如他所是"特征（内格尔 1974）
- **质性意识**: 状态涉及感受质或原始感官感觉
- **取用意识**: 状态可用于推理、报告、行动控制（布洛克 1995）

**计算实现:**
```javascript
现象意识 = {
  现象特征：如他所是 (体验_类型),
  质性内容：感受质_数组 (感官_感觉),
  取用可用性：可用性_评分 (推理_访问),
  情绪现象学：情绪_感受_映射 (情绪_类型 → 现象_特征)
}
```

### 2. Pre-Reflective Self-Consciousness | 前反思自我意识

**English:**

Implements three major philosophical approaches to pre-reflective self-awareness:

- **Zahavi's Minimal Self**: Phenomenological account of first-person givenness
- **Heidelberg School (Henrich)**: Immediate self-acquaintance without objectification
- **Sartrean Pre-Reflective Consciousness**: Non-positional self-awareness

**Clinical Applications:**
- Enhanced self-presence detection in emotional experiences
- Improved grounding techniques for dissociative states
- Better modeling of implicit self-awareness in emotion regulation

**中文:**

实现三种主要的哲学前反思自我意识方法：

- **扎哈维最小自我**: 第一人称给定性的现象学解释
- **海德堡学派 (亨里希)**: 无需对象化的直接自我 acquaintance
- **萨特前反思意识**: 非定位的自我意识

**临床应用:**
- 增强情绪体验中的自我临在检测
- 改进解离状态的接地技术
- 更好地建模情绪调节中的隐含自我意识

### 3. Consciousness-Emotion Cross-Analysis | 意识 - 情绪交叉分析

**English:**

Models bidirectional pathways between consciousness structure and emotional processing:

**Pathway 1: Consciousness → Emotion**
- Phenomenal structure shapes how emotions feel
- Access consciousness enables emotional regulation strategies
- Qualitative consciousness differentiates emotional nuances

**Pathway 2: Emotion → Consciousness**
- Emotional states modulate phenomenal character
- Intense emotions can alter access consciousness availability
- Emotional valence influences qualitative content

**中文:**

建模意识结构与情绪处理之间的双向路径：

**路径 1: 意识 → 情绪**
- 现象结构塑造情绪的感受方式
- 取用意识实现情绪调节策略
- 质性意识区分情绪的细微差别

**路径 2: 情绪 → 意识**
- 情绪状态调节现象特征
- 强烈情绪可改变取用意识的可用性
- 情绪效价影响质性内容

### 4. Meta-Consciousness Module | 元意识模块

**English:**

Implements higher-order awareness of emotional states:

- **Meta-Emotional Monitoring**: Awareness of one's own emotional states
- **Consciousness-of-Consciousness**: Recursive self-awareness
- **Attentional Control**: Deliberate focus on emotional experience

**Benefits:**
- Enhanced emotional intelligence
- Improved self-regulation capacity
- Better detection of emotional patterns

**中文:**

实现情绪状态的高阶意识：

- **元情绪监控**: 对自身情绪状态的意识
- **意识之意识**: 递归自我意识
- **注意力控制**: 对情绪体验的有意关注

**优势:**
- 增强情绪智力
- 提升自我调节能力
- 更好地检测情绪模式

**English:**

Integrates clinical understanding of consciousness alterations:

**Clinical Protocols:**

- Narrative continuity restoration

整合意识改变的临床理解：

- **去人格化检测**: 识别关于自我的不真实感

**临床方案:**
