### 🧠 Theoretical Integration | 理论集成

#### 5 New SEP Theories | 5 个新 SEP 理论

1. **Consciousness Theory | 意识理论**
   - Creature consciousness, state consciousness, phenomenal consciousness
   - 生物意识、状态意识、现象意识
   - Qualia tracking enhanced to 12-bit
   - 感质追踪增强至 12 位

2. **Self-Consciousness Theory | 自我意识理论**
   - Cartesian cogito, Kantian transcendental apperception
   - 笛卡尔我思、康德先验统觉
   - Implicit/explicit self-awareness spectrum
   - 隐性/显性自我意识谱系

3. **Intentionality Theory | 意向性理论**
   - Five-level intentionality framework
   - 五层意向性框架
   - Brentano's thesis, intentional inexistence
   - 布伦塔诺论题、意向性内存在

4. **Emotion Theory | 情绪理论**
   - Three traditions integration (Feeling, Evaluative, Motivational)
   - 三传统集成（感觉、评价、动机）
   - Six-component emotion model
   - 六成分情绪模型

5. **Phenomenal Structure | 现象结构**
   - Spatial-temporal-conceptual organization
   - 时空 - 概念组织
   - Enhanced unity of consciousness
   - 增强的意识统一性
