### 3. Collective Intentionality: We-Intention Deep Structure | 集体意向性：我们意向深层结构

**Source | 来源**: SEP Collective Intentionality (2026)

#### Individual Ownership vs. Irreducibility | 个体所有 vs. 不可还原性
- **Central Problem**: Distribution of individual intentions ≠ collective intention
- **核心问题**: 个体意向的分布 ≠ 集体意向
- **Irreducibility Claim**: Collective intentionality not reducible to individual + common knowledge
- **不可还原性主张**: 集体意向性不可还原为个体 + 共同知识
- **Individual Ownership Thesis**: Each individual has their own mind/brain
- **个体所有论题**: 每个个体有自己的心灵/大脑

#### Sellars-Bratman-Tuomela Synthesis | 塞拉斯 - 布兰特曼 - 图梅拉综合
- **We-Intention Structure**: I intend that we J framework
- **我们意向结构**: 我意图我们 J 框架
- **Mutual Responsiveness**: Interconnected intention formation
- **相互响应**: 相互连接的意向形成
- **Mode Account**: We-mode vs. I-mode distinction (Tuomela 2007)
- **模式账户**: 我们模式与我模式区分 (Tuomela 2007)

#### Phenomenological Collective Emotion | 现象学集体情绪
- **Scheler 1954**: Numerically identical shared emotional states
- **谢勒 1954**: 数值相同的共享情绪状态
- **Example**: Parents' shared grief at child's deathbed - one state, two minds
- **示例**: 父母在子女病床前的共享悲伤 - 一个状态，两个心灵
- **Walther 1923**: Four-layer shared experience model
- **瓦尔特 1923**: 四层共享体验模型
  1. A experiences x, B experiences x / A 体验 x，B 体验 x
  2. A empathizes with B's experience, vice versa / A 共情 B 的体验，反之亦然
  3. A identifies with B's experience, vice versa / A 认同 B 的体验，反之亦然
  4. Mutual empathetic awareness of identification / 对认同的相互共情意识

#### Trust-Based Collective Intentionality | 基于信任的集体意向性
- **Schmid 2013 Model**: Cognitive + normative dual components
- **施密德 2013 模型**: 认知 + 规范双重成分
- **Trust Attitude**: Combines reliance + normative expectation
- **信任态度**: 结合依赖 + 规范期望
- **Application**: Trust framework analysis in relational emotions
- **应用**: 关系情绪中的信任框架分析

#### Social Theory Foundations | 社会理论基础
- **Durkheim 1898**: Collective consciousness explanation of social facts
- **涂尔干 1898**: 社会事实的集体意识解释
- **Weber 1922**: Individual intentional attitudes with shared aims
- **韦伯 1922**: 具有共享目标的个体意向态度
- **Collingwood 1947**: Society as joint will - "We will go for this walk"
- **柯林伍德 1947**: 社会作为联合意志 - "我们将去散步"

## Theoretical Challenges Addressed | 解决的理论挑战

### 1. Emotion Differentiation Problem | 情绪区分问题

**Challenge | 挑战**: How to distinguish emotions from non-emotions and from each other  
**挑战**: 如何区分情绪与非情绪以及情绪之间的区别

**Solution | 解决方案**:
- Prototype structure (Fehr & Russell 1984) with typicality scoring
- 原型结构 (Fehr & Russell 1984) 带典型性评分
- Five-component matching: evaluative, physiological, phenomenological, expressive, behavioral
- 五成分匹配：评估、生理、现象学、表达、行为
- Confidence calibration based on component match degree
- 基于成分匹配度的置信度校准
- Borderline cases handling (e.g., boredom - split intuition)
- 边界案例处理 (如无聊 - 直觉分裂)

### 2. Recalcitrance to Reason | 对理性的抗拒

**Challenge | 挑战**: Emotions persist despite contradictory judgments  
**挑战**: 尽管有矛盾判断，情绪仍然持续

**Solution | 解决方案**:
- Perceptual theory integration: emotions as evaluative perceptions
- 知觉理论整合：情绪作为评估性知觉
- Cognitive impenetrability explanation (Tappolet 2016)
- 认知不可渗透性解释 (Tappolet 2016)
- Analogy to visual illusions: passive assent vs. active judgment
- 类比视觉错觉：被动同意 vs. 主动判断
- Phenomenological validation: emotional experience autonomy
- 现象学验证：情绪体验自主性

### 3. Individual Ownership vs. Irreducibility | 个体所有 vs. 不可还原性

**Challenge | 挑战**: Collective intentionality must be both individual and irreducible  
**挑战**: 集体意向性必须既是个体的又是不可还原的

**Solution | 解决方案**:
- Mode account integration: we-mode vs. I-mode distinction (Tuomela 2007)
- 模式账户整合：我们模式与我模式区分 (Tuomela 2007)
- Relational self-conception: subject-in-relations framework
- 关系性自我概念：关系中的主体框架
- Collective commitment with individual autonomy preservation
- 保持个体自主性的集体承诺
- Trust-based representation: cognitive + normative components (Schmid 2013)
- 基于信任的表征：认知 + 规范成分 (施密德 2013)

### 4. Theoretical Kinds vs. Folk Categories | 理论种类 vs. 民间范畴

**Challenge | 挑战**: Folk emotion categories heterogeneous - natural kinds?  
**挑战**: 民间情绪范畴异质 - 是自然种类吗？

**Solution | 解决方案**:
- Distinguish descriptive vs. prescriptive definitions
- 区分描述性 vs. 规定性定义
- Ordinary language compatibility + theoretical fruitfulness balance
- 日常语言兼容性 + 理论成果平衡
- Conceptual explication strategy (Carnap 1950)
- 概念阐明策略 (卡尔纳普 1950)
- Prototype structure acceptance with typicality gradients
- 接受原型结构带典型性梯度
