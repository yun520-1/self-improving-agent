### 4. Instrumental Rationality Framework v5 | 工具理性框架 v5

**Source | 来源**: SEP Instrumental Rationality (2024 edition), practical philosophy

**Key Components | 核心组件**:
- **Rational Coherence | 理性一致性**: Internal consistency of attitudes and actions
- **Reason vs Ought | 理由与应当**: Distinction between what one has reason to do vs what one ought to do
- **Instrumental Transmission | 工具传递**: Raz's Facilitative Principle, reason transmission from ends to means
- **Desire-Based Theories | 基于欲望的理论**: Subjective vs Objective Desire-Based Theories
- **Value-Based Theories | 基于价值的理论**: Independent value constraints on rational ends

**Transmission Principles | 传递原则**:
- **Ought Necessity**: If ought to E, and M is necessary means, then ought to M
- **Strong Reasons Necessity**: If reason to E, and M is necessary means, then as strong reason to M
- **Weak Reasons Necessity**: If reason to E, and M is necessary means, then some reason to M
- **Cost Sensitivity**: Means costs can outweigh transmitted reasons
