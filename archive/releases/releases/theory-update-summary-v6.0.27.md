## 📐 New Theorems | 新增定理

### Theorem WB.4: Emotion-Wellbeing Constitution | 情绪 - 福祉构成定理

> **Theorem**: Emotions are constitutive elements of well-being, not merely instrumental means to well-being.

**Proof Sketch**:
1. Well-being includes hedonic dimension (from WB.2)
2. Emotions are partly constitutive of hedonic experience (from E.1-E.8)
3. Well-being includes objective list dimension (from WB.2)
4. Emotional virtues (e.g., appropriate anger, compassion) are objective goods (from Virtue Ethics SEP)
5. Therefore, emotions are constitutive of well-being

**HeartFlow Application**:
- Emotion work framed as well-being enhancement, not just symptom reduction
- Assessment includes well-being impact of emotional patterns
- Intervention goals include both hedonic and eudaimonic outcomes

### Theorem H.5: Happiness-Wellbeing Alignment | 幸福 - 福祉对齐定理

> **Theorem**: Psychological happiness (emotional flourishing) and eudaimonic happiness (well-being) are positively correlated but not identical.

**Proof Sketch**:
1. Psychological happiness is emotional state (from H.3)
2. Eudaimonic happiness is well-being/flourishing (from H.3, WB.1-WB.3)
3. Emotional flourishing contributes to but doesn't exhaust well-being (from WB.2)
4. Well-being enhancement tends to improve emotional state (from WB.4)
5. Therefore, positive correlation without identity

**HeartFlow Application**:
- User education distinguishes mood vs. flourishing goals
- Progress tracking shows both metrics
- Interventions target alignment between state and flourishing

### Theorem H.6: Life Satisfaction Mediation | 生活满意度中介定理

> **Theorem**: Life satisfaction mediates the relationship between emotional coherence and well-being assessment.

**Proof Sketch**:
1. Emotional coherence improves emotional state (from P.1-P.7)
2. Emotional state influences life evaluation (from H.4)
3. Life satisfaction contributes to well-being (from H.4, WB.2)
4. Therefore, life satisfaction mediates emotion→well-being pathway

**HeartFlow Application**:
- Life satisfaction module added to intervention pathway
- Cognitive restructuring targets global life evaluation
- Satisfaction tracking across life domains

### Theorem SC.5: Pre-Reflective Foundation | 前反思基础定理

> **Theorem**: Reflective self-consciousness (explicit self-thought) presupposes pre-reflective self-awareness (implicit self-presence).

**Proof Sketch**:
1. Reflective self-consciousness takes self as object (from Self-Consciousness SEP)
2. For reflection to recognize object as self, pre-reflective self-acquaintance required (from Fichte, Heidelberg School)
3. Pre-reflective awareness is immediate, non-objectifying (from Sartre, phenomenology)
4. Therefore, pre-reflective awareness is foundational

**HeartFlow Application**:
- Mindfulness training targets pre-reflective awareness
- Emotion awareness exercises distinguish pre-reflective vs. reflective modes
- Self-model development progresses from implicit to explicit

### Theorem SC.6: Intersubjective Necessity | 主体间必要性定理

> **Theorem**: Full self-consciousness requires intersubjective recognition and social interaction.

**Proof Sketch**:
1. Self-consciousness requires awareness of oneself as objective entity (from Kant, Strawson)
2. Objective self-awareness develops through other's perspective (from Mead, Hegel, Fichte)
3. Intersubjective recognition validates self-conception (from SC.3, OM.1)
4. Therefore, intersubjective interaction is necessary for full self-consciousness

**HeartFlow Application**:
- Social emotion scenarios include self-development dimension
- Group therapy module leverages intersubjective recognition
- Empathy training bidirectional (self-through-other)
