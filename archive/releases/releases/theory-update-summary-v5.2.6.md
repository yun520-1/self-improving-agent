## 1. Emotion Theory: Three Traditions Deep Integration | 情绪理论：三大传统深度整合

### 1.1 Theoretical Background | 理论背景

**Source | 来源**: SEP Emotion (Scarantino 2016, 2026) §2-3

The SEP entry identifies **three broad traditions** in emotion theory:

SEP 条目识别情绪理论中的**三大传统**：

## 2. Self-Consciousness: Dual-Pathway Model | 自我意识：双通路模型

### 2.1 Theoretical Background | 理论背景

**Source | 来源**: SEP Self-Consciousness §1-4

Key distinction: **Intuitive Self-Knowledge** vs. **Inferential Self-Knowledge**

关键区分：**直觉式自我知识** vs. **推论式自我知识**

- **Intuitive (Non-Inferential) | 直觉式（非推论）**:
  - Direct, immediate awareness (Descartes' cogito, Locke's intuitive knowledge)
  - 直接、即时觉察（笛卡尔的 cogito，洛克的直觉知识）
  - "I am thinking" known without inference
  - "我在思考"无需推理即可知

- **Inferential | 推论式**:
  - Based on observation, evidence, reasoning (Hume's bundle theory)
  - 基于观察、证据、推理（休谟的束理论）
  - Self as constructed from perceptions
  - 自我从感知中建构

## 3. Collective Intentionality: Irreducibility Framework | 集体意向性：不可还原性框架

### 3.1 Theoretical Background | 理论背景

**Source | 来源**: SEP Collective Intentionality §1-2

**Central Problem | 核心问题**:
- Collective intentions are NOT just sums of individual intentions
- 集体意向性不仅仅是个体意向的总和
- **Irreducibility Claim**: "We intend" ≠ "I intend + You intend + Common Knowledge"
- **不可还原性主张**："我们意向"≠"我意向 + 你意向 + 共同知识"

**Individual Ownership Thesis | 个体所有权论题**:
- Each individual has their own mind
- 每个个体都有自己的心智
- No "collective super-brain"
- 不存在"集体超级大脑"

**Tension | 张力**: How to reconcile irreducibility with individual ownership?
如何调和不可还原性与个体所有权？

## 4. Phenomenological Shared Experience: Walther-Scheler Integration | 现象学共享经验：瓦尔特 - 舍勒整合

### 4.1 Theoretical Synthesis | 理论综合

**Walther's Four-Layer Model | 瓦尔特的四层模型**:
- Requires mutual empathetic awareness at each layer
- 每层都需要相互共情觉察
- **Strength | 优势**: Explicit, testable conditions
- **明确、可测试的条件**
- **Weakness | 弱点**: Infinite regress problem (why stop at layer 4?)
- **无限倒退问题（为何停在第 4 层？）**

**Scheler's Single-State Model | 舍勒的单一状态模型**:
- Collective emotion is ONE state shared by many minds
- 集体情绪是多个心智共享的一个状态
- **Strength | 优势**: Avoids infinite regress, captures phenomenological unity
- **避免无限倒退，捕捉现象学统一性**
- **Weakness | 弱点**: Metaphysically controversial (how can one state be in many minds?)
- **形而上学争议（一个状态如何存在于多个心智中？）**
