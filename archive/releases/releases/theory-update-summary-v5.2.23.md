## Theoretical Foundations | 理论基础

### 1. Enactivism | 生成主义

**Source | 来源**: SEP Embodied Cognition (2026) + Varela et al. (1991) + Thompson (2007) + Di Paolo & Thompson (2014)

#### 1.1 Core Principles | 核心原则

**Core Claim | 核心主张**: Cognition is not the representation of a pregiven world by a pregiven mind, but rather the enactment of a world and a mind through embodied action.  
认知不是预先给定的心灵对预先给定的世界的表征，而是通过具身行动生成一个世界和一个心灵。

**The Four E's of Cognition | 认知的四个 E**:
- **Embodied | 具身的**: Cognition depends on bodily experience and sensorimotor capacities  
  认知依赖于身体经验和感觉运动能力
- **Embedded | 嵌入的**: Cognition occurs in real-world environments with affordances and constraints  
  认知发生在具有 affordances 和约束的真实世界环境中
- **Enactive | 生成的**: Cognition brings forth a meaningful world through active engagement  
  认知通过主动参与带出一个有意义的世界
- **Extended | 延展的**: Cognition extends beyond the brain into body and environment  
  认知超越大脑延伸到身体和环境
