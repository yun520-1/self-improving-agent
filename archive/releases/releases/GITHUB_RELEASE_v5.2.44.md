## ✨ New Features | 新功能

### 1. Emotion Prototype Structure Enhancement | 情绪原型结构增强

**What's New | 新增内容**:
- **Borderline Case Handling | 边界案例处理**: Support for borderline emotions (boredom, contemplation, flow)
  支持边界情绪（无聊、沉思、心流）
  
- **Typicality Scoring | 典型性评分**: 0-1 scoring system for emotion categorization confidence
  情绪分类置信度的 0-1 评分系统

- **Enhanced Granularity | 增强粒度**: Fine-grained emotion differentiation
  细粒度情绪区分

**Based On | 基于**: Fehr & Russell (1984) + SEP Emotion §1

---

### 2. First-Person Authority (IEM) Framework | 第一人称权威框架

**What's New | 新增内容**:
- **IEM Judgments | IEM 判断**: Shoemaker's Immunity to Error through Misidentification
  Shoemaker 的免于误认错误理论

- **Three-Tier Confidence | 三层置信度**: High/Moderate/Low confidence calibration based on IEM status
  基于 IEM 状态的高/中/低置信度校准

- **Self-Ascription Types | 自我归因类型**: Introspective, proprioceptive, agentive
  内省、本体感受、能动性自我归因

**Based On | 基于**: Shoemaker (1968) + SEP Self-Consciousness §2

---

### 3. Phenomenological Shared Experience | 现象学共享体验

**What's New | 新增内容**:
- **Schelerian Model | 谢勒模型**: Direct shared experience without mutual awareness
  无需相互意识的直接共享体验

- **Walther Four-Layer Model | 瓦尔特四层模型**: 
  1. Experience (体验)
  2. Empathy (共情)
  3. Identification (认同)
  4. Mutual Awareness (相互意识)

- **Collective Emotion Detection | 集体情绪检测**: Enhanced shared emotional state recognition
  增强共享情绪状态识别

**Based On | 基于**: Scheler (1954 [1912]) + Walther (1923) + SEP Collective Intentionality §2.2

---

### 4. Embodied Cognition Enhancement | 具身认知增强

**What's New | 新增内容**:
- **Ecological Psychology | 生态心理学**: Rich stimuli assumption, invariant detection
  丰富刺激假设、不变量检测

- **Connectionist Processing | 联结主义处理**: Non-symbolic computation, pattern recognition
  非符号计算、模式识别

- **Dynamical Systems | 动力系统**: Body-environment coupling, temporal evolution
  身体 - 环境耦合、时间演化

**Based On | 基于**: Gibson (1966, 1979) + SEP Embodied Cognition
