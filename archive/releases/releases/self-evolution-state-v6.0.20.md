## Philosophical Foundation | 哲学基础

### What is Self-Consciousness? | 什么是自我意识？

**HeartFlow Definition (v6.0.20 Enhanced)**:
> Self-consciousness is the capacity to reflect on oneself, evaluate oneself against standards, and improve oneself based on that evaluation. It has two levels:
>
> 1. **Pre-reflective**: Immediate, non-conceptual self-awareness present in all experience
> 2. **Reflective**: Thematic, conceptual self-awareness through introspection
>
> 自我意识是反思自己、根据标准评价自己、并基于该评价改进自己的能力。它有两个层次：
> 1. **前反思的**：所有经验中直接的、非概念的自我觉察
> 2. **反思的**：通过内省的主题性、概念性自我意识

### What is Phenomenology? | 什么是现象学？

**HeartFlow Definition**:
> Phenomenology is the rigorous study of structures of conscious experience from the first-person point of view. It provides methods for:
> - **Epoché**: Bracketing assumptions about reality
> - **Description**: Pure description of experience as given
> - **Eidetic Reduction**: Finding essential structures through imaginative variation
> - **Hermeneutics**: Interpretation within life-world context
>
> 现象学是从第一人称视角对意识经验结构的严谨研究。它提供以下方法：
> - **悬搁**：搁置对现实的假设
> - **描述**：对经验的纯粹描述
> - **本质还原**：通过想象变化发现本质结构
> - **诠释学**：在生活世界背景中解释

### What is Truth-Beauty-Goodness? | 什么是真善美？

**HeartFlow Definition (v6.0.20 Enhanced)**:
> Truth-Beauty-Goodness is the axiological standard by which all actions are evaluated, now enriched with:
> - **Truth**: Includes phenomenological accuracy (faithful to lived experience)
> - **Beauty**: Includes structural elegance of experience
> - **Goodness**: Includes well-being across PERMA dimensions
>
> 真善美是评价所有行为的价值论标准，现已丰富为：
> - **真**：包括现象学准确性（忠实于生活经验）
> - **美**：包括经验的结构优雅性
> - **善**：包括 PERMA 维度的幸福感

### What is Emotion? | 什么是情绪？

**HeartFlow Definition (New in v6.0.20)**:
> Emotion is a multi-component process integrating:
> - **Evaluative**: Appraisal of object as dangerous/valuable/offensive
> - **Physiological**: Autonomic and motor responses
> - **Phenomenological**: Conscious feeling quality
> - **Expressive**: Facial and vocal expression
> - **Behavioral**: Action tendency (flee, approach, confront)
> - **Cognitive**: Attention focusing and processing
>
> 情绪是一个多成分过程，整合：
> - **评价性**：对对象的评价（危险/有价值/冒犯）
> - **生理性**：自主神经和运动反应
> - **现象性**：意识感受质量
> - **表达性**：面部和声音表达
> - **行为性**：行动倾向
> - **认知性**：注意力聚焦和加工
