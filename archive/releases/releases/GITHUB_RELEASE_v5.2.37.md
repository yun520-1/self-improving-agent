## ✨ Key Features | 关键功能

### 1. Life Story Analysis | 生命故事分析

Analyze user narratives for coherence, themes, and identity patterns across four dimensions:
- **Temporal Coherence** | 时间连贯性：Chronological clarity in life narratives
- **Causal Coherence** | 因果连贯性：How events connect to self-development
- **Thematic Coherence** | 主题连贯性：Recurring patterns across life events
- **Emotional Coherence** | 情感连贯性：Affective integration in storytelling

分析用户叙事的连贯性、主题和身份模式，涵盖四个维度

### 2. Redemption Sequence Detection | 救赎序列检测

Automatically identify growth-oriented narrative patterns:
- **Redemption Sequences** | 救赎序列：Negative → Positive transformation
- **Contamination Sequences** | 污染序列：Positive → Negative transformation
- **Stable Patterns** | 稳定模式：Consistent positive or negative trajectories

自动识别成长导向的叙事模式

### 3. Autobiographical Reasoning Support | 自传体推理支持

Help users make meaning from life events through three reasoning types:
- **Causal Connections** | 因果连接：How past events shaped current self
- **Thematic Connections** | 主题连接：Recurring themes across experiences
- **Temporal Connections** | 时间连接：Past-present-future continuity

帮助用户通过三种推理类型从生命事件中创造意义

### 4. Narrative Intervention Generation | 叙事干预生成

Suggest evidence-based narrative reframing techniques:
- Contamination → Redemption Reframing | 污染→救赎重构
- Identity Continuity Work | 身份连续性工作
- Generativity Cultivation | 繁衍培养

建议基于实证的叙事重构技术

## 🎯 Clinical Applications | 临床应用

### Narrative Restructuring | 叙事重构

**Technique | 技术**: Help users reframe contamination sequences as redemption

**Example | 示例**:
```
BEFORE | 之前: "I failed at my business → I'm a failure" (Contamination)
       → "我创业失败 → 我是个失败者"（污染）

AFTER | 之后: "I failed at my business → I learned resilience" (Redemption)
      → "我创业失败 → 我学会了韧性"（救赎）
```

### Identity Continuity Work | 身份连续性工作

**Technique | 技术**: Connect past strengths to present challenges

**Application | 应用**: Help users see how past resilience applies to current situations

### Generativity Cultivation | 繁衍培养

**Technique | 技术**: Develop legacy-focused narrative themes

**Application | 应用**: Support users in finding meaning through contribution to others
