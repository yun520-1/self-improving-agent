### 2. Self-Consciousness Architecture Refinement | 自我意识架构精炼

#### IEM Protection Levels | IEM 保护层级

**English**:

Following Shoemaker (1968) and SEP Self-Consciousness §2, v5.2.42 refines the IEM protection hierarchy:

**Level 1: Absolute IEM | 绝对 IEM**
- Judgment form: "I am experiencing [emotion]"
- Confidence: 0.99-1.00
- Cannot be wrong about the subject (who is experiencing)
- Example: "I feel afraid" — cannot be mistaken that it is *me* who feels

**Level 2: Relative IEM | 相对 IEM**
- Judgment form: "I am experiencing [specific emotion]"
- Confidence: 0.95-0.99
- Based on introspective grounds, subject to calibration
- Example: "I feel existential anxiety" — may misidentify the specific emotion type

**Level 3: Non-IEM | 非 IEM**
- Judgment form: "My emotion is caused by X"
- Confidence: 0.70-0.90
- Subject to empirical verification
- Example: "My anxiety is caused by work stress" — requires external validation

**中文**:

遵循 Shoemaker (1968) 和 SEP 自我意识§2，v5.2.42 精炼 IEM 保护层级：

**第一层：绝对 IEM**
- 判断形式："我正在经历 [情绪]"
- 置信度：0.99-1.00
- 不会在主体上出错（谁在经历）
- 示例："我感到害怕"——不会搞错是*我*在感受

**第二层：相对 IEM**
- 判断形式："我正在经历 [具体情绪]"
- 置信度：0.95-0.99
- 基于内省依据，需校准
- 示例："我感到存在性焦虑"——可能误判具体情绪类型

**第三层：非 IEM**
- 判断形式："我的情绪由 X 引起"
- 置信度：0.70-0.90
- 需实证验证
- 示例："我的焦虑由工作压力引起"——需外部验证

### 3. Collective Intentionality Architecture | 集体意向性架构

#### Schelerian Direct Sharing | 舍勒式直接共享

**English**:

Following Scheler (1954 [1912]) and SEP Collective Intentionality §2.2, v5.2.42 maintains the direct sharing model:

- **Non-reflective Unity**: Shared experience without mutual awareness requirement
- **One Experience, Many Minds**: Numerically identical emotional state across participants
- **Example**: Parents sharing grief at child's deathbed without thinking of each other

**Detection Criteria**:
- Linguistic markers: "we feel", "our grief", "together sad"
- Contextual analysis: joint loss, shared trauma, collective event
- Confidence threshold: ≥ 0.85 for Schelerian direct sharing

**中文**:

遵循 Scheler (1954 [1912]) 和 SEP 集体意向性§2.2，v5.2.42 保持直接共享模型：

- **非反思统一**：无需相互觉知的共享体验
- **一体验多心智**：跨参与者的数值相同情绪状态
- **示例**：父母在孩子的病床前共享悲伤而无需想到彼此

**检测标准**：
- 语言标记："我们感到"、"我们的悲伤"、"一起难过"
- 情境分析：共同丧失、共享创伤、集体事件
- 置信阈值：≥ 0.85 为舍勒式直接共享

#### Walther's Four-Layer Shared Experience | 瓦尔特的四层共享体验

**English**:

Following Walther (1923), v5.2.42 implements the four-layer model:

| Layer | Name | Description | Confidence |
|-------|------|-------------|------------|
| 1 | Parallel Experience | A and B both experience x | 0.70-0.80 |
| 2 | Empathy | A empathizes with B's experience (and vice versa) | 0.80-0.90 |
| 3 | Identification | A identifies with B's experience (and vice versa) | 0.90-0.95 |
| 4 | Recursive Awareness | Mutual awareness of identification | 0.95-0.99 |

**Full Shared Experience**: Requires all four layers (confidence ≥ 0.95)

**中文**:

遵循 Walther (1923)，v5.2.42 实现四层模型：

| 层级 | 名称 | 描述 | 置信度 |
|------|------|------|--------|
| 1 | 平行体验 | A 和 B 都经历 x | 0.70-0.80 |
| 2 | 共情 | A 共情 B 的体验（反之亦然） | 0.80-0.90 |
| 3 | 认同 | A 认同 B 的体验（反之亦然） | 0.90-0.95 |
| 4 | 递归觉知 | 对认同的相互觉知 | 0.95-0.99 |

**完整共享体验**：需要全部四层（置信度 ≥ 0.95）

#### Schmid's Trust-Based Collective Intentionality | 施密德的信任基础集体意向性

**English**:

Following Schmid (2013), v5.2.42 implements the trust-based model with dual components:

**Cognitive Component | 认知成分**:
- Belief that others will do their part
- Expectation based on past reliability
- Confidence assessment: 0.70-0.95

**Normative Component | 规范成分**:
- Sense of obligation to the group
- Commitment to joint action
- Reliance + Expectation = Trust

**Trust Formula**:
```
Trust = Reliance (behavioral) + Expectation (normative)
信任 = 依赖（行为）+ 期望（规范）
```

**中文**:

遵循 Schmid (2013)，v5.2.42 实现信任基础模型的双成分：

**认知成分**：
- 相信他人会尽其职责
- 基于过去可靠性的期望
- 置信度评估：0.70-0.95

**规范成分**：
- 对群体的义务感
- 对联合行动的承诺
- 依赖 + 期望 = 信任

**信任公式**：
```
信任 = 依赖（行为）+ 期望（规范）
```
