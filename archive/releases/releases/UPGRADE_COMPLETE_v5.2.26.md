## Key Theoretical Additions | 关键理论新增

### 1. Emotion Prototype Theory | 情绪原型理论

**Source | 来源**: Fehr & Russell (1984) + SEP Emotion §1

- **Graded Membership | 分级成员**: Some emotion instances are better examples than others
- **Borderline Cases | 边缘案例**: Boredom, curiosity, surprise identified as borderline emotions
- **Multi-Dimensional Typicality | 多维度典型性**: Valence, arousal, motivation, expression, physiology

### 2. First-Person Authority | 第一人称权威

**Source | 来源**: Shoemaker (1968) + SEP Self-Consciousness §2

- **IEM (Immunity to Error Through Misidentification) | 免于误认错误**: Some self-ascriptions cannot be wrong about who is feeling
- **Judgment Types | 判断类型**: Interoceptive (immune), Observational (not immune), Testimonial (not immune)
- **Confidence Calibration | 置信度校准**: IEM judgments receive higher confidence scores

### 3. Trust-Based Collective Intentionality | 信任基础的集体意向性

**Source | 来源**: Schmid (2013) + SEP Collective Intentionality §2.2

- **Trust Model | 信任模型**: Cognitive component (belief) + Normative component (expectation)
- **Walther-Scheler Synthesis | Walther-Scheler 综合**: Four-level shared experience + irreducibly collective emotions
- **Shared Experience Quality | 共享体验质量**: Trust assessment enables better collective emotion detection

### 4. Aesthetic Emotion Framework | 审美情绪框架

**Source | 来源**: SEP Aesthetic Emotions + Schindler et al. (2017)

- **Six Aesthetic Emotions | 六种审美情绪**: Beauty, Interest, Curiosity, Sublime, Nostalgia, Awe
- **Appraisal Patterns | 评估模式**: Each emotion has distinct cognitive appraisal profile
- **Action Tendencies | 行动倾向**: Each emotion motivates distinct behaviors
