- Five integration levels from "Disconnected" (20%) to "Wisdom" (100%)

#### 3. Temporal-Self Integration | 时间 - 自我整合 (98.0%)

**Based on | 基于**: SEP Temporal Consciousness + Husserl + William James

- **显似现在（威廉·詹姆斯）**: 具有 2-30 秒时间厚度的体验"现在"
- Temporal self-continuity across past, present, and future
