## What's New | 新增内容

### 4 Major Theories Integrated | 4 个主要理论整合

1. **Attention Theory | 注意理论**
   - Epistemic role (Descartes): Attention enables certainty
   - Abstraction mechanism (Berkeley, Kant): Withholding attention for abstract thought
   - Mode of thinking (Locke): Attention, intention, reverie
   - Psychological integration: Perception, action, reflective thought

2. **Imagination Theory | 想象理论**
   - Attitude vs. Imagistic imagining (Van Leeuwen)
   - Constructive imagination (process over time)
   - Spontaneous vs. Deliberate (Walton)
   - Imagination as Cognitive Skill (Kind 2020)
   - Recreative Theory (Currie & Ravenscroft 2002)
   - Imagination as Process (Wiltsher 2023)

3. **Aesthetic Experience Theory | 审美体验理论**
   - Disinterested pleasure (Kant)
   - Formalism vs. Cognitivism
   - Aesthetic emotions (wonder, awe, sublime, beauty)

4. **Mental Imagery Theory | 心理意象理论**
   - Multi-modal formats (visual, auditory, tactile, etc.)
   - Imagery-perception overlap (simulation theory)
   - Individual differences (aphantasia to hyperphantasia)
