## New Theoretical Insights | 新理论洞见

### 1. Consciousness-Emotion Relationship | 意识 - 情绪关系

**Insight | 洞见**: Emotion episodes require different levels of consciousness depending on complexity.

**Explanation | 解释**:
- **Minimal consciousness** (Aquinas): Sufficient for basic emotion feelings / 足以支持基本情绪感受
- **Reflective consciousness** (Aristotle): Required for complex emotion appraisal / 复杂情绪评价所需
- **Phenomenal structure** (Kant): Emotions situated in structured experience / 情绪定位于结构化体验中

**Application | 应用**: HeartFlow now calibrates emotion analysis depth based on detected consciousness level.

---

### 2. Collective Emotion Ontology | 集体情绪本体论

**Insight | 洞见**: Shared emotions are irreducible to individual emotions + common knowledge.

**Explanation | 解释**:
- **Irreducibility**: We-grief ≠ my-grief + your-grief + mutual knowledge / 我们悲伤 ≠ 我的悲伤 + 你的悲伤 + 相互知识
- **Collective attribution**: Intention belongs to participants collectively, not distributively / 意向属于参与者集体而非分配地
- **Joint commitment**: Creates mutual obligation for emotional attunement / 创建情绪协调的相互义务

**Application | 应用**: Enhanced detection and modeling of trust-based emotional bonding.
