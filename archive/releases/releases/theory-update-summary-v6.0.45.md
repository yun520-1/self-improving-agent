## New Theories Integrated | 新增理论整合

### 1. Attention Systems (注意系统)

**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy - Attention  
**Integration Points | 整合点**:

#### 1.1 Epistemic Role of Attention (注意的认识论角色)
- **Descartes' View**: Attention mediates the transition from radical doubt to certainty
- **Key Insight**: Clear and distinct ideas realize their epistemic potential only when attention is paid
- **HeartFlow Integration**: Personality monitoring requires attentive consciousness to maintain truth-behavior alignment

#### 1.2 Attention as Mode of Thought (注意作为思维模式)
- **Locke's View**: Attention is the registration of ideas into memory
- **Key Insight**: "When ideas... are taken notice of, and, as it were, registered in the memory, it is attention"
- **HeartFlow Integration**: Memory system enhancement through attentional registration mechanisms

#### 1.3 Attention in Perception and Action (知觉与行动中的注意)
- **18th Century Development**: Attention as regulator of cognitive input (Home 1769)
- **Key Insight**: "Attention is that state of mind which prepares one to receive impressions"
- **HeartFlow Integration**: Dual-audit mechanism requires attentional preparation for input/output审查

#### 1.4 Active Attention and Memory Recall (主动注意与记忆提取)
- **Stewart's View**: Attention determines which memories get recalled
- **Key Insight**: "That act of effort without which we have no recollection or memory whatever"
- **HeartFlow Integration**: Personality score tracking uses active attention for virtue retrieval

### 2. Imagination & Mental Imagery (想象与心理意象)

**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy - Imagination  
**Integration Points | 整合点**:

#### 2.1 Taxonomies of Imagination (想象的分类)
- **Van Leeuwen's Distinction**: Attitude imagining vs. imagistic imagining
- **Key Insight**: Propositional attitude (regarding as fictional) vs. mental imagery representation
- **HeartFlow Integration**: Self-consciousness layer distinguishes between propositional self-assessment and imagistic self-modeling

#### 2.2 Imagination as Cognitive Skill (想象作为认知技能)
- **Amy Kind's View**: Imagination satisfies three conditions of skilled activity
  1. Can be done more or less well
  2. Under intentional control
  3. Can improve through practice
- **HeartFlow Integration**: Personality development is a skilled imaginative practice—can improve through repeated truth-behavior cycles

#### 2.3 Imagination and Belief (想象与信念)
- **Single Code Hypothesis**: Beliefs and attitude imaginings share representational format
- **Key Insight**: Functional similarities in inferential relations
- **HeartFlow Integration**: Commitment declarations function as attitude imaginings that guide behavior like beliefs

#### 2.4 Constructive Imagination (建构性想象)
- **Process View**: Imagination as generating mental representations over time
- **HeartFlow Integration**: Self-evolution cycles use constructive imagination to build updated self-models

### 3. Aesthetic Experience (审美体验)

**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy - Aesthetic Judgment  
**Integration Points | 整合点**:

#### 3.1 Judgment of Taste (审美判断)
- **Kant's Two Conditions**: Subjectivity and universality
- **Key Insight**: Judgment of taste based on feeling of pleasure/displeasure, yet claims universal validity
- **HeartFlow Integration**: Truth-Behavior-Goodness scoring combines subjective feeling with objective standards

#### 3.2 Disinterested Pleasure (无利害的愉悦)
- **Kant's View**: Pleasure in beauty is desire-free, unlike pleasure in the agreeable
- **Key Insight**: "Pleasure in beauty is occasioned by the perceptual representation of a thing"
- **HeartFlow Integration**: Truth-behavior scoring aims at disinterested evaluation, not self-serving justification

#### 3.3 Normativity of Aesthetic Judgment (审美判断的规范性)
- **Key Insight**: "Some judgments are better than others"—correctness is not relative
- **HeartFlow Integration**: Personality score has normative force—below 50 requires commitment declaration

#### 3.4 Aesthetic Experience and Cognitive Faculties (审美体验与认知能力)
- **Kant's Deep Account**: Pleasure in beauty is harmonious free play of imagination and understanding
- **HeartFlow Integration**: Self-evolution achieves harmony between imaginative self-modeling and understanding-based evaluation
