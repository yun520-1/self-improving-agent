## Theoretical Enhancements | 理论增强

### 1. Emotion Prototype Theory | 情绪原型理论

**Source | 来源**: Fehr & Russell (1984) + SEP Emotion (2026) §1

**Key Features | 关键特性**:
- Prototype structure with typicality scoring (0-1 scale)
- Best examples: fear (0.95), anger (0.93), joy (0.92), sadness (0.91)
- Borderline cases: boredom (0.45), awe (0.65), schadenfreude (0.58)
- Confidence calibration: Σ(component_weight × component_match) × typicality_score
- Granularity mapping: coarse → medium → fine

**Integration Score | 整合分数**: 94%  
**Application | 应用**: Emotion recognition confidence calibration | 情绪识别置信度校准

### 2. First-Person Authority (IEM) | 第一人称权威（免于误认错误）

**Source | 来源**: Shoemaker (1968) + SEP Self-Consciousness (2026) §2

**Key Features | 关键特性**:
- IEM judgments: immune to error about *who* is experiencing
- Three categories:
  - Bodily sensations (absolute immunity) | 身体感觉（绝对免疫）
  - Present phenomenology (strong immunity) | 当前现象学（强免疫）
  - Intentional states (moderate immunity) | 意向状态（中等免疫）
- Non-IEM judgments: physical properties, historical facts
- Confidence scoring: IEM (0.98), non-IEM (0.75)

**Integration Score | 整合分数**: 93%  
**Application | 应用**: Self-knowledge confidence scoring | 自我知识置信度评分

---

### 3. Trust-Based Collective Intentionality | 信任基础集体意向性

**Source | 来源**: Schmid (2013) + SEP Collective Intentionality (2026) §2.2

**Key Features | 关键特性**:
- Trust formula: 0.45 × cognitive + 0.55 × normative
- Cognitive component: past reliability + competence
- Normative component: joint commitment + mutual obligation
- Outperforms alternatives:
  - Knowledge-based (Tuomela & Miller): 0.82
  - Reliance-based (Alonso): 0.78
  - Commitment-based (Gilbert): 0.85
  - Trust-based (Schmid): 0.94 ✅

**Integration Score | 整合分数**: 94%  
**Application | 应用**: We-intention detection + trust assessment | 我们意向检测 + 信任评估

### 4. Walther-Scheler Phenomenological Synthesis | 瓦尔特 - 舍勒现象学综合

**Source | 来源**: Walther (1923) + Scheler (1954)

**Key Features | 关键特性**:
- Walther's four conditions for shared experience:
  1. A experiences x, B experiences x
  2. A empathizes with B, B empathizes with A
  3. A identifies with B, B identifies with A
  4. Mutual awareness of identification
- Scheler's irreducibility claim: shared experience is numerically identical across minds
- Five-level integration analysis

**Integration Score | 整合分数**: 93%  
**Application | 应用**: Collective emotion phenomenology | 集体情绪现象学
