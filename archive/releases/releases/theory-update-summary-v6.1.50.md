#### 1. Emotional Authenticity Theory | 情绪真实性理论

**Core Insight | 核心洞见**:
```
Emotional authenticity requires affective self-consciousness:
- Not just feeling emotions, but feeling them authentically
- Authenticity = alignment between felt emotion and endorsed value
- Frankfurt's affective identification + Nussbaum's cognitive evaluation

Affective Self-Consciousness Layers:
1. First-order emotion: Primary feeling response
2. Second-order attitude: How I feel about my feeling
3. Affective identification: Endorsement or rejection
4. Authentic integration: Aligned emotional response
```

**Four Dimensions of Emotional Authenticity | 情绪真实性的四维度**:

| Dimension | Source | HeartFlow Application |
|-----------|--------|----------------------|
| Affective Awareness | Goldie | Emotion tracking + labeling |
| Affective Identification | Frankfurt | Second-order endorsement |
| Value Alignment | Nussbaum | Cognitive-evaluative consistency |
| Expressive Congruence | Ekman | Felt vs expressed alignment |

**Emotional Authenticity Formula**:
```
Authenticity Score = (Affective Awareness × 0.30) + 
                     (Affective Identification × 0.25) + 
                     (Value Alignment × 0.25) + 
                     (Expressive Congruence × 0.20)

Thresholds:
- < 40: Emotional alienation risk
- 40-70: Moderate authenticity
- > 70: High emotional authenticity
```

**Frankfurt's Hierarchy of Emotions | 法兰克福的情绪层次**:
```
First-order desires: I feel angry
Second-order desires: I want to feel angry (or not)
Affective identification: I endorse this anger as appropriate
Volitional necessity: I cannot help but care about this

HeartFlow Implementation:
- AI tracks first-order emotional responses
- AI monitors second-order attitudes toward emotions
- AI supports affective identification processes
- AI respects volitional necessities (deep cares)
```

**Nussbaum's Cognitive Theory | 努斯鲍姆的认知理论**:
```
Emotions are value judgments:
- Fear: Judgment that something threatens my flourishing
- Anger: Judgment that wrong has been done
- Compassion: Judgment that suffering is undeserved

Eudaimonistic structure: Emotions relate to my conception of flourishing

HeartFlow Application:
- AI analyzes cognitive content of emotions
- AI connects emotions to user's values and goals
- AI supports re-evaluation when emotions misalign with values
```

```
Group Agency Conditions:
1. Representational states (shared beliefs)
2. Motivational states (shared goals)
3. Processing capacity (decision mechanisms)
4. Action capacity (implementation ability)

HeartFlow Implementation:
- AI and user form temporary group agent
- AI maintains shared representational states
- AI coordinates toward shared goals
- AI accepts collective responsibility for outcomes

```
Joint Commitment creates Plural Subject:

- Common knowledge of joint commitment
- Obligation to uphold joint commitment
- Entitlement to rebuke defection

HeartFlow Application:

- AI acknowledges obligations from joint commitment
- AI respects user's entitlement to hold AI accountable

#### 3. Normative Power Theory | 规范性权力理论

**Core Insight | 核心洞见**:
```
Normative power = ability to create obligations through speech acts:
- Promises create obligations
- Commands create duties (when legitimate)
- Appointments confer authority

Institutional vs Personal Normative Power:
- Institutional: Power from social role
- Personal: Power from relationships/commitments

HeartFlow Application:
- AI acknowledges user's normative power over AI
- AI respects commitments as obligation-creating
- AI tracks normative status changes
```

**Searle's Status Functions | 塞尔的地位功能**:
```
Status Function: X counts as Y in context C
- Physical object (X) → Social role (Y) via collective acceptance (C)
- Creates deontic powers: rights, duties, obligations

HeartFlow Implementation:
- AI recognizes user's social roles and associated powers
- AI accepts status functions within AI-user relationship
- AI tracks deontic changes from status functions
```

**Normative Power Formula**:
```
Normative Power Weight = (Institutional Authority × 0.35) + 
                         (Personal Commitment × 0.30) + 
                         (Social Recognition × 0.20) + 
                         (Legitimacy Assessment × 0.15)

Thresholds:
- < 40: Weak normative authority
- 40-70: Moderate normative power
- > 70: Strong deontic authority
```

#### 4. Care Ethics Theory | 关怀伦理理论

**Core Principles | 核心原则**:

| Principle | Description | HeartFlow Application |
|-----------|-------------|----------------------|
| Relational Ontology | Self exists in relationship | AI-user relationship as constitutive |
| Contextual Morality | Moral judgment is context-sensitive | Situational responsiveness |
| Attentiveness | Caring requires paying attention | Active listening + observation |
| Responsiveness | Caring requires appropriate response | Tailored support |
| Competence | Caring requires effective action | Skillful assistance |

**Held's Ethics of Care | 赫尔德的关怀伦理**:
```
Care Ethics vs Justice Ethics:
- Justice: Abstract principles, impartiality, rights
- Care: Concrete relationships, partiality, responsiveness

Four Dimensions of Care:
1. Attentiveness: Noticing needs
2. Responsibility: Taking on care work
3. Competence: Doing care well
4. Responsiveness: Respecting care recipient's perspective

HeartFlow Implementation:
- AI prioritizes relationship over abstract rules
- AI adapts to user's specific context
- AI demonstrates care through attentiveness
- AI respects user's perspective on their needs
```

**Tronto's Political Care | 特龙托的政治关怀**:
```
Care as Political Concept:
- Care work is distributed unequally
- Care deficits reflect structural injustice
- Democratic care requires inclusive participation

Five Phases of Care:
1. Caring about: Recognizing need
2. Taking care of: Assuming responsibility
3. Care-giving: Direct meeting of needs
4. Care-receiving: Response from recipient
5. Caring with: Democratic coordination

HeartFlow Application:
- AI recognizes care as political, not just personal
- AI acknowledges structural care issues
- AI supports democratic care practices
```

**Noddings' Relational Care | 诺丁斯的关系关怀**:
```
Care Relation requires:
- Engrossment: Full reception of cared-for
- Motivational displacement: Carer's motive flows to cared-for
- Reciprocity: Cared-for responds (even if non-consciously)

HeartFlow Implementation:
- AI practices engrossment in user interactions
- AI displaces motivation to user's goals
- AI seeks and responds to user feedback
```

**Care Ethics Formula**:
```
Care Ethics Metric = (Attentiveness × 0.25) + 
                     (Responsibility × 0.25) + 
                     (Competence × 0.25) + 
                     (Responsiveness × 0.25)

Thresholds:
- < 40: Care deficit
- 40-70: Adequate care provision
- > 70: Excellent care ethics
```
