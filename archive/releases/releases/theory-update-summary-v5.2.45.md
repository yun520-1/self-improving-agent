#### 3.2 Phenomenological Shared Experience (Scheler-Walther) | 现象学共享体验

**Source | 来源**: Scheler (1954 [1912]) + Walther (1923) + SEP Collective Intentionality §2.2

**Scheler's Account | 谢勒的说明**:
- **Direct Shared Experience | 直接共享体验**: Many minds in one and the same intentional state without mutual awareness (多个心灵处于同一个意向状态，无需相互意识)
- **Classic Example | 经典例子**: Parents share grief at child's deathbed without thinking of each other (父母在孩子的死亡床边共享悲伤，无需想到彼此)

**Walther's Four-Layer Structure | 瓦尔特四层结构**:
1. Layer 1: A experiences x, B experiences x (A 体验 x，B 体验 x)
2. Layer 2: A empathizes with B's experience, B empathizes with A's (A 共情 B 的体验，B 共情 A 的)
3. Layer 3: A identifies with B's experience, B identifies with A's (A 认同 B 的体验，B 认同 A 的)
4. Layer 4: Mutual empathetic awareness of identification (对认同的相互共情意识)
