## 🔬 New Theories | 新增理论

### 1. Temporal Identity Theory | 时间身份理论

**Core Insight**: Identity extends through time via memory, narrative, commitment, and anticipation.

**Key Components**:
- Episodic Memory (Locke): Past experiences as mine
- Narrative Coherence (Ricoeur): Life-story integration
- Commitment Persistence (Korsgaard): Practical identity over time
- Anticipatory Projection (Heidegger): Future-oriented self

**Formula**: Temporal Coherence Score (27th formula)

### 2. Collective Intentionality Theory | 集体意向性理论

**Core Insight**: We-intentions enable genuine collaboration beyond parallel individual actions.

**Key Components**:
- We-Intention (Searle): Shared intention with collective content
- We-Mode vs I-Mode (Tuomela): Collective vs individual orientation
- Mutual Belief: Shared understanding of collective goal
- Collective Commitment: Joint obligation to follow through

**Formula**: Collective Intention Index (28th formula)

### 3. Normative Authority Theory | 规范性权威理论

**Core Insight**: AI authority is earned through user reflective endorsement, not assumed.

**Key Components**:
- Reflective Endorsement (Korsgaard): Authority through reflection
- Practical Identity Support: Respecting user's roles and commitments
- Second-Personal Accountability (Darwall): Mutual address and responsibility
- Moral Identity Alignment: Humanity as ultimate practical identity

**Formula**: Normative Authority Weight (29th formula)

### 4. Recognition Justice Theory | 承认正义理论

**Core Insight**: Justice requires both redistribution (material) and recognition (symbolic).

**Key Components**:
- Bivalent Conception (Fraser): Redistribution + Recognition
- Affirmative vs Transformative: Correcting vs changing structures
- Five Faces of Oppression (Young): Exploitation, Marginalization, Powerlessness, Cultural Imperialism, Violence
- Social Connection Model: Shared responsibility for structural conditions

**Formula**: Recognition Justice Metric (30th formula)
