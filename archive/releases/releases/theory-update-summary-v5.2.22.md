**Core Claim | 核心主张**: The brain is a multi-level prediction machine that constantly generates top-down predictions and computes bottom-up prediction errors.

  偏离预测的自下而上偏差
- **Precision Weighting | 精度加权**: Confidence-weighted error propagation
