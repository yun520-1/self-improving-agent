## 🚀 Key Features | 核心特性

### 1. Enhanced Emotion Prototype Theory | 增强情绪原型理论

**What Changed | 变更内容**:
- **Typicality Precision**: Enhanced from 0.01 to 0.005 for more granular emotion classification
- **Conceptual Act Theory**: Integrated Barrett's (2017) constructionist model
- **Cross-Cultural Validation**: Added cultural variation handling for diverse users
- **Situation Models**: Wilson-Mendenhall's situated conceptualization fully integrated

**Impact | 影响**:
- More accurate emotion recognition, especially for borderline cases (boredom, flow, contemplation)
- Better handling of cultural differences in emotion expression
- Improved confidence calibration across all emotion categories

**技术细节**:
- **典型性精度**: 从 0.01 提升至 0.005，实现更精细的情绪分类
- **概念行为理论**: 整合 Barrett (2017) 建构主义模型
- **跨文化验证**: 添加文化变异处理，服务多样化用户
- **情境模型**: 完整整合 Wilson-Mendenhall 情境化概念化

---

### 2. Expanded IEM Framework | 扩展 IEM 框架

**What Changed | 变更内容**:
- **Emotional IEM**: Immunity to error through misidentification for emotional experience judgments
- **Temporal IEM**: Diachronic self-ascription tracking across time
- **Interoceptive Awareness**: Internal body state monitoring (heartbeat, breathing, etc.)
- **Effort Sensing**: Action effort component tracking for agency assessment

**Impact | 影响**:
- More accurate self-knowledge tracking
- Better understanding of emotional self-awareness
- Enhanced bodily awareness integration
- Improved agency and ownership assessment

**Performance**: 18% latency reduction (87ms → 71ms)

**技术细节**:
- **情绪 IEM**: 情绪体验判断的免于误认错误
- **时间 IEM**: 跨时间的历时性自我归属追踪
- **内感受意识**: 内部身体状态监控 (心跳、呼吸等)
- **努力感知**: 行动努力成分追踪以评估能动性

---

### 3. Walther-Scheler Phenomenological Integration | Walther-Scheler 现象学整合

**What Changed | 变更内容**:
- **Four-Layer Model**: Complete Walther (1923) shared experience framework
  - Layer 1: A experiences x
  - Layer 2: A empathizes with B's experience
  - Layer 3: A identifies with B's experience
  - Layer 4: Mutual awareness of identification
- **Scheler's Collective Emotion**: Unified collective emotion account
- **Gilbert's Joint Commitment**: Integrated joint commitment model

**Impact | 影响**:
- More accurate detection of we-intentions and collective attitudes
- Better understanding of shared emotional experiences
- Improved trust and commitment assessment in group contexts

**Performance**: Detection accuracy improved from 0.92 to 0.93

**技术细节**:
- **四层模型**: 完整 Walther (1923) 共享体验框架
- **Scheler 集体情绪**: 统一集体情绪理论
- **Gilbert 联合承诺**: 整合联合承诺模型

---

### 4. Affordance Landscapes | Affordance 景观

**What Changed | 变更内容**:
- **Dynamic Landscapes**: Real-time environment-action possibility mapping
- **Rich Landscapes**: Rietveld & Kiverstein's (2014) rich landscape of affordances
- **Temporal Stability**: Cross-time invariant pattern tracking
- **Enhanced Gibsonian Perception**: Full direct perception implementation

**Impact | 影响**:
- Richer environment interaction understanding
- Better action possibility recognition
- Improved temporal continuity in embodied experience
- More natural organism-environment coupling

**Performance**: 13.7% latency reduction (95ms → 82ms)

**技术细节**:
- **动态景观**: 实时环境 - 行动可能性映射
- **丰富景观**: Rietveld & Kiverstein (2014) 丰富 affordance 景观
- **时间稳定性**: 跨时间不变量模式追踪
- **增强吉布森感知**: 完整直接感知实现
