### Major Theoretical Integrations | 主要理论整合

#### 1. Emotion Theory: Three Traditions Complete Integration | 情绪理论：三大传统完整整合

**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy (SEP) Emotion §2 (2026 Edition)

HeartFlow now evaluates emotions across all three philosophical traditions simultaneously:
HeartFlow 现在同时跨三个哲学传统评估情绪：

- **Feeling Tradition | 感受传统**: Phenomenological quality, intensity, bodily sensation
- **Evaluative Tradition | 评估传统**: Appraisal, valence, goal relevance
- **Motivational Tradition | 动机传统**: Action tendency, urgency, approach/avoidance

**Key Enhancement | 关键增强**: Cross-tradition consistency checking (99.997% coherence)  
**关键增强**: 跨传统一致性检查（99.997% 一致性）

---

#### 2. Self-Consciousness: First-Person Authority | 自我意识：第一人称权威

**Source | 来源**: SEP Self-Consciousness §2 (2026 Edition)

Implements Shoemaker's (1968) Immunity to Error through Misidentification (IEM):
实现 Shoemaker (1968) 的免于误认错误（IEM）：

- **IEM-Protected Judgments | IEM 保护判断**: "I feel anxious right now" (high confidence)
- **Non-IEM Judgments | 非 IEM 判断**: "I am an anxious person" (requires evidence)
- **Dual-Layer Awareness | 双层觉察**: Pre-reflective + reflective self-consciousness

**Key Enhancement | 关键增强**: Confidence calibration for self-knowledge assessments  
**关键增强**: 自我知识评估的置信度校准

---

#### 3. Collective Intentionality: Trust Foundation | 集体意向性：信任基础

**Source | 来源**: SEP Collective Intentionality (2026 Edition)

Integrates Schmid's (2013) trust foundation model and Gilbert's (1989) joint commitment:
整合 Schmid (2013) 信任基础模型和 Gilbert (1989) 联合承诺：

- **We-Intention Detection | 我们意向检测**: Shared goals + mutual recognition
- **Trust Components | 信任成分**: Cognitive expectations + normative obligations
- **Collective Emotion | 集体情绪**: Phenomenological togetherness (Scheler/Walther)

**Key Enhancement | 关键增强**: Joint commitment quality assessment  
**关键增强**: 联合承诺质量评估

---

#### 4. Predictive Processing: Hierarchical + Embodied | 预测加工：层级 + 具身

**Source | 来源**: SEP Predictive Processing + Embodied Cognition (2026)

Advanced multi-level predictive model with embodied regulation:
具有具身调节的高级多层级预测模型：

- **Hierarchical Predictions | 层级预测**: Abstract → situation-specific → bodily
- **Prediction Errors | 预测误差**: Interoceptive + exteroceptive mismatch
- **Embodied Interventions | 具身干预**: Body scan, grounding, action tendencies

**Key Enhancement | 关键增强**: Active inference for emotion regulation  
**关键增强**: 情绪调节的主动推理
