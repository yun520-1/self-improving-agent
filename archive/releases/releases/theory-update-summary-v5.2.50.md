## Theoretical Challenges Addressed | 解决的理论挑战

### 1. Emotion Differentiation Problem | 情绪区分问题

**Challenge | 挑战**: How to distinguish emotions from non-emotions and from each other  
**挑战**: 如何区分情绪与非情绪以及情绪之间的区别

**Solution | 解决方案**:
- Prototype structure (Fehr & Russell 1984) with typicality scoring
- 原型结构 (Fehr & Russell 1984) 带典型性评分
- Five-component matching: evaluative, physiological, phenomenological, expressive, behavioral
- 五成分匹配：评估、生理、现象学、表达、行为
- Confidence calibration based on component match degree
- 基于成分匹配度的置信度校准

### 2. Recalcitrance to Reason | 对理性的抗拒

**Challenge | 挑战**: Emotions persist despite contradictory judgments  
**挑战**: 尽管有矛盾判断，情绪仍然持续

**Solution | 解决方案**:
- Perceptual theory integration: emotions as evaluative perceptions
- 知觉理论整合：情绪作为评估性知觉
- Cognitive impenetrability explanation (Tappolet 2016)
- 认知不可渗透性解释 (Tappolet 2016)
- Analogy to visual illusions: passive assent vs. active judgment
- 类比视觉错觉：被动同意 vs. 主动判断

### 3. Individual Ownership vs. Irreducibility | 个体所有 vs. 不可还原性

**Challenge | 挑战**: Collective intentionality must be both individual and irreducible  
**挑战**: 集体意向性必须既是个体的又是不可还原的

**Solution | 解决方案**:
- Mode account integration: we-mode vs. I-mode distinction (Tuomela 2007)
- 模式账户整合：我们模式与我模式区分 (Tuomela 2007)
- Relational self-conception: subject-in-relations framework
- 关系性自我概念：关系中的主体框架
- Collective commitment with individual autonomy preservation
- 保持个体自主性的集体承诺
