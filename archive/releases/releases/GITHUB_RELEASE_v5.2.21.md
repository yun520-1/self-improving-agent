## Release Highlights | 发布亮点

### English

**HeartFlow v5.2.21** represents a significant theoretical deepening in emotional AI architecture, integrating three major philosophical frameworks:

1. **Emotion Prototype Theory** (Fehr & Russell 1984): Introduces typicality-based emotion recognition with confidence calibration and granularity mapping.

2. **First-Person Authority** (Shoemaker 1968): Implements immunity to error through misidentification (IEM) judgments for self-knowledge validation.

3. **Trust-Based Collective Intentionality** (Schmid 2013): Develops a cognitive-normative dual-component trust model for we-intention detection.

4. **Walther-Scheler Phenomenological Synthesis**: Integrates four-level shared experience analysis with irreducible unity assessment.

## Key Features | 关键特性

### 1. Emotion Prototype Theory | 情绪原型理论

**English**:
- Prototype structure with typicality scoring (0-1 scale)
- Best examples: fear (0.95), anger (0.93), joy (0.92), sadness (0.91)
- Borderline cases: boredom (0.45), awe (0.65), schadenfreude (0.58)
- Confidence calibration formula: Σ(component_weight × component_match) × typicality_score
- Granularity mapping: coarse → medium → fine

**中文**:
- 含典型性评分的原型结构（0-1 量表）
- 最佳例子：恐惧 (0.95)、愤怒 (0.93)、喜悦 (0.92)、悲伤 (0.91)
- 边界案例：无聊 (0.45)、敬畏 (0.65)、幸灾乐祸 (0.58)
- 置信度校准公式：Σ(成分权重 × 成分匹配) × 典型性评分
- 粒度映射：粗 → 中 → 细

---

### 2. First-Person Authority (IEM) | 第一人称权威（免于误认错误）

**English**:
- IEM judgments: immune to error about *who* is experiencing
- Three categories: bodily sensations (absolute), present phenomenology (strong), intentional states (moderate)
- Non-IEM judgments: physical properties, historical facts
- Confidence scoring: IEM (0.98), non-IEM (0.75)

**中文**:
- IEM 判断：免于关于*谁*在体验的错误
- 三个类别：身体感觉（绝对）、当前现象学（强）、意向状态（中等）
- 非 IEM 判断：物理属性、历史事实
- 置信度评分：IEM (0.98)、非 IEM (0.75)

---

### 3. Trust-Based Collective Intentionality | 信任基础集体意向性

**English**:
- Trust formula: 0.45 × cognitive_expectation + 0.55 × normative_commitment
- Cognitive component: past reliability + competence assessment
- Normative component: joint commitment + mutual obligation
- Outperforms alternatives: knowledge-based (0.82), reliance-based (0.78), commitment-based (0.85)

**中文**:
- 信任公式：0.45 × 认知期望 + 0.55 × 规范承诺
- 认知成分：过去可靠性 + 能力评估
- 规范成分：联合承诺 + 相互义务
- 优于替代方案：知识基础 (0.82)、依赖基础 (0.78)、承诺基础 (0.85)

---

### 4. Walther-Scheler Synthesis | 瓦尔特 - 舍勒综合

**English**:
- Walther's four conditions for shared experience
- Scheler's irreducibility claim (numerically identical across minds)
- Five-level integration analysis
- Application to collective emotion phenomenology

**中文**:
- 瓦尔特的共享体验四个条件
- 舍勒的不可还原主张（跨心灵数值相同）
- 五层整合分析
- 应用于集体情绪现象学
