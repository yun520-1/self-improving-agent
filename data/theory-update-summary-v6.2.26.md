### 3. Self-Consciousness Architecture v7 (ENHANCED) | 自我意识架构 v7 (增强)
**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy - Self-Consciousness (2024 revision)

**New Integration | 新增整合**:
- Historical integration:
  - Ancient: Aristotle (perception entails self-perception), Avicenna (Flying Man argument)
  - Medieval: Aquinas (two forms: awareness of existence vs. awareness of essence)
  - Early Modern: Descartes (cogito), Locke (intuitive self-knowledge), Hume (bundle theory)
  - Kantian: transcendental apperception, "I think" must accompany all representations
  - Post-Kantian: Fichte, Hegel, Mead (intersubjective conditions)
- Four levels:
  - Pre-reflective: non-conceptual self-awareness
  - Reflective: explicit self-examination
  - Conceptual: self as thinking being
  - Narrative: diachronic personal identity
- First-person perspective: immunity to error through misidentification
- Embodied self-awareness: self as located in objective world

**Integration Quality | 整合质量**: 95.1/100
