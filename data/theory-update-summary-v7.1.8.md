6. **Intentional states**: Object-directed consciousness

## 2. Embodied Cognition Framework v4 | 具身认知框架 v4

### 2.1 Three Themes | 三大主题

**Source | 来源**: SEP Embodied Cognition, Merleau-Ponty (1945, 1962), Gallagher (2005)

**Theme 1: Conceptualization | 概念化**:
- Body properties limit/constrain concepts
- Differently embodied organisms acquire different concepts
- Sensory-motor systems shape conceptual content

**Theme 2: Replacement | 替代**:
- Body-environment interaction can REPLACE internal computation
- Ecological psychology: rich stimuli, no inference needed
- Dynamical systems > symbolic computation

**Theme 3: Constitution | 构成**:
- Body CONSTITUTES cognition (not just influences)
- Phenomenology: consciousness is embodied
- Mind-body problem is illusory
