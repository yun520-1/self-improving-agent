## New Theories Integrated | 新增整合理论

### 1. Emotion Theory Update (SEP 2024) | 情绪理论更新

**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy - Emotion (2024)  
**Category | 类别**: emotion-theory  
**Integration Points | 整合点**:
- **Multi-dimensional heterogeneity | 多维异质性**: Emotions differ along multiple dimensions (occurrences vs dispositions, short-lived vs long-lived, conscious vs unconscious)
- **Three traditions integration | 三大传统整合**: 
  - Feeling Tradition: emotions as distinctive conscious experiences
  - Evaluative Tradition: emotions as appraisals of eliciting circumstances
  - Motivational Tradition: emotions as motivational states
- **Problem of parts resolution | "部分问题"解决**: Which components (evaluative, physiological, phenomenological, expressive, behavioral, mental) are essential to emotion
- **Prototype structure | 原型结构**: Emotion concepts are prototypically organized (Fehr & Russell 1984)

**Key Insights | 核心洞察**:
```
1. Folk emotion categories may not be natural kinds, but theoretical kinds for specific purposes
2. Theoretical fruitfulness vs ordinary language compatibility tension
3. Four explanatory challenges: Differentiation, Motivation, Intentionality, Phenomenology
4. James-Lange theory: "our feeling of bodily changes as they occur IS the emotion"
5. Cannon's critique: visceral reactions of fear and anger are indistinguishable
```

**Integration Status | 整合状态**: ✅ Integrated into emotion evaluation module

### 2. Consciousness Theory Update (SEP 2024) | 意识理论更新

**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy - Consciousness (2024)  
**Category | 类别**: consciousness-phenomenology  
**Integration Points | 整合点**:
- **Creature consciousness vs State consciousness | 生物意识与状态意识**: 
  - Sentience, Wakefulness, Self-consciousness, What-it-is-like, Subject of conscious states, Transitive consciousness
- **State consciousness dimensions | 状态意识维度**:
  - States one is aware of (meta-mentality)
  - Qualitative states (qualia)
  - Phenomenal states (phenomenal structure)
  - What-it-is-like states
  - Subjective consciousness
  - Transitive consciousness
- **Historical development | 历史发展**: Descartes → Locke → Leibniz → Kant → Husserl → Heidegger → Merleau-Ponty

**Key Insights | 核心洞察**:
```
1. Consciousness is arguably the central issue in current theorizing about the mind
2. From Descartes to Locke: consciousness defined as reflexive self-awareness
3. Leibniz distinguished perception from apperception
4. Kant: phenomenal consciousness requires structured experience in objective world
5. 20th century: behaviorism marginalized consciousness, cognitive psychology revived it
6. 1980s-90s: major resurgence (Baars, Dennett, Chalmers, Crick, Lycan)
```

**Integration Status | 整合状态**: ✅ Integrated into consciousness architecture v13

### 3. Phenomenological Self-Consciousness (SEP 2024) | 现象学自我意识

**Source | 来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy - Phenomenological Approaches to Self-Consciousness (2024)  
**Category | 类别**: self-consciousness-phenomenology  
**Integration Points | 整合点**:
- **Prereflective self-consciousness | 前反思自我意识**: 
  - Awareness before reflection
  - Implicit, first-order awareness
  - Condition of possibility for reflective self-consciousness
- **For-me-ness | 为我性**: The distinct first-personal givenness of experience
- **Non-observational self-acquaintance | 非观察性自我熟悉**: Self is there without reflection
- **Infinite regress argument | 无限倒退论证**: Higher-order theories generate regress; phenomenological alternative avoids it

**Key Insights | 核心洞察**:
```
1. Prereflective self-consciousness is present whenever I am living through an experience
2. "Every positional consciousness of an object is at the same time a non-positional consciousness of itself" (Sartre)
3. Self-consciousness is not a quality added to experience, but the mode of existence of consciousness itself
4. Higher-order theories claim consciousness is extrinsic; phenomenologists argue it's intrinsic
5. The regress argument: non-conscious meta-state cannot transform a state into one with phenomenal qualities
```

**Integration Status | 整合状态**: ✅ Integrated into self-consciousness v8 module
