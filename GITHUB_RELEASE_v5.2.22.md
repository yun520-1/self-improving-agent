## Key Features | 关键特性

### 1. Predictive Processing (Enhanced) | 预测加工（增强）

**English**:
- Four-level hierarchical prediction: sensory (ms), perceptual (s), conceptual (min), narrative (hrs-days)
- Precision weighting: confidence-weighted prediction error propagation
- Neuromodulator mapping: dopamine (action), acetylcholine (sensory), norepinephrine (unexpected uncertainty), serotonin (expected uncertainty)
- Emotion as predictive construction: interoceptive + exteroceptive + conceptual predictions
- Integration with emotion prototype theory: typicality informs prediction priors

**中文**:
- 四层层级预测：感觉（毫秒）、知觉（秒）、概念（分钟）、叙事（小时 - 天）
- 精度加权：置信度加权的预测误差传播
- 神经调节剂映射：多巴胺（行动）、乙酰胆碱（感觉）、去甲肾上腺素（意外不确定性）、血清素（预期不确定性）
- 情绪作为预测建构：内感受 + 外感受 + 概念预测
- 与情绪原型理论整合：典型性告知预测先验

---

### 2. Active Inference | 主动推理

**English**:
- Expected free energy formula: G = 0.55×Risk + 0.25×Ambiguity + 0.10×Novelty + 0.10×Valence
- Risk: divergence from preferred outcomes (55% weight)
- Ambiguity: uncertainty about outcomes (25% weight)
- Novelty: surprise about states (10% weight)
- Valence: prior preference strength (10% weight)
- Policy selection: softmax over negative expected free energy
- Three policy types: epistemic (information gathering), pragmatic (goal achievement), affiliative (relationship building)
- Multi-horizon planning: short (1-step), medium (3-step), long (10-step)

**中文**:
- 期望自由能公式：G = 0.55×风险 + 0.25×模糊性 + 0.10×新颖性 + 0.10×效价
- 风险：偏离偏好结果（55% 权重）
- 模糊性：结果不确定性（25% 权重）
- 新颖性：状态意外（10% 权重）
- 效价：先验偏好强度（10% 权重）
- 策略选择：负期望自由能的 softmax
- 三种策略类型：认知性（信息收集）、实用性（目标达成）、亲和性（关系建立）
- 多视野规划：短期（1 步）、中期（3 步）、长期（10 步）

---

### 3. Embodied Cognition (Deepened) | 具身认知（深化）

**English**:
- Sensorimotor contingencies: lawful relations between movement and sensory feedback
- Four contingency types:
  - Visual: eye movement → retinal image change
  - Interoceptive: breathing → cardiac rhythm → emotional feeling
  - Proprioceptive: posture → muscle tension → emotional state
  - Social: facial expression → other's response → social feedback
- Body-environment coupling across timescales:
  - Milliseconds: sensorimotor loops
  - Seconds: gesture-speech coordination
  - Minutes: emotional attunement
  - Hours-Days: habit formation
- Affordance detection: emotional affordances (comfort, threat, opportunity)
- Enactive approach: cognition as embodied action (4Es: Embodied, Embedded, Enactive, Extended)

**中文**:
- 感觉运动偶连：运动与感觉反馈之间的规律关系
- 四种偶连类型：
  - 视觉：眼球运动 → 视网膜图像变化
  - 内感受：呼吸 → 心律 → 情绪感受
  - 本体感受：姿势 → 肌肉紧张 → 情绪状态
  - 社会：面部表情 → 他人反应 → 社会反馈
- 跨时间尺度的身体 - 环境耦合：
  - 毫秒：感觉运动环路
  - 秒：手势 - 言语协调
  - 分钟：情绪调谐
  - 小时 - 天：习惯形成
- 可供性检测：情绪可供性（舒适、威胁、机会）
- 生成主义方法：认知作为具身行动（4E：具身的、嵌入的、生成的、延展的）

---

### 4. Free Energy Principle | 自由能原理

**English**:
- Variational free energy formula: F = Ep(s|ψ)[ln q(ψ) - ln p(s,ψ)]
- Interpretation: surprise about sensory states approximated via recognition density
- Three minimization strategies:
  - Perceptual inference: update beliefs to match sensations (ms-s)
  - Active inference: act to change sensations (s-min)
  - Learning: update generative model (min-days)
- Unified framework for emotion regulation and therapeutic change
- Connection to active inference: expected free energy guides action selection

**中文**:
- 变分自由能公式：F = Ep(s|ψ)[ln q(ψ) - ln p(s,ψ)]
- 解释：通过识别密度近似的关于感觉状态的意外
- 三种最小化策略：
  - 知觉推理：更新信念以匹配感觉（毫秒 - 秒）
  - 主动推理：行动以改变感觉（秒 - 分钟）
  - 学习：更新生成模型（分钟 - 天）
- 情绪调节和治疗变化的统一框架
- 与主动推理的连接：期望自由能引导行动选择
