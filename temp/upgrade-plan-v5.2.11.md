### 2. Self-Consciousness Theory (SEP 2026) | 自我意识理论

**Key Insights | 关键洞察**:
- **Dual-Pathway Model**: Intuitive vs. Inferential self-knowledge distinction
- **Pre-reflective Awareness**: Immediate, non-inferential self-consciousness
- **First-Person Authority**: High certainty for intuitive self-knowledge
- **Fallible Reflection**: Inferential self-knowledge subject to correction

**Integration Points | 集成点**:
- Enhance self-consciousness-dual-pathway-v5.2.10 with conflict detection
- Add calibration mechanism for self-knowledge confidence
- Integrate phenomenological pre-reflective awareness module

### 3. Collective Intentionality (SEP 2026) | 集体意向性理论

**Key Insights | 关键洞察**:
- **Irreducibility Thesis**: Collective intentionality not reducible to individual intentions
- **We-Intention**: Searle/Gilbert/Bratman models of shared intention
- **Walther's Four Layers**: Progressive depth of shared experience
- **Scheler's Sympathy Model**: Fellow feeling vs. emotional infection distinction

**Integration Points | 集成点**:
- Enhance collective-emotion-phenomenology-v5.2.10 with layer detection
- Add authenticity check for genuine vs. performative collective emotion
