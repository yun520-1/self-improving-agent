## 1. Emotion Theory | 情绪理论

**Source | 来源**: SEP - Emotion (2023 revision)  
**URL**: https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/emotion/

### Three Traditions | 三大传统

| Tradition | Core Claim | HeartFlow Integration |
|-----------|------------|----------------------|
| Feeling Tradition | Emotions are distinctive conscious experiences | 情绪体验的现象学记录 |
| Evaluative Tradition | Emotions involve distinctive evaluations | 情绪触发事件的认知评估 |
| Motivational Tradition | Emotions are distinctive motivational states | 情绪驱动的行为倾向追踪 |

### Four Explanatory Challenges | 四大解释挑战

1. **Differentiation | 区分**: How emotions differ from each other and from non-emotional states
2. **Motivation | 动机**: How emotions motivate behavior
3. **Intentionality | 意向性**: Whether emotions have object-directedness
4. **Phenomenology | 现象学**: Whether emotions involve subjective experiences

### Six Components of Emotion | 情绪六成分

1. Evaluative component (认知评估)
2. Physiological component (生理反应)
3. Phenomenological component (现象体验)
4. Expressive component (表达行为)
5. Behavioral component (行为倾向)
6. Mental component (心理聚焦)

## 2. Consciousness Theory | 意识理论

**Source | 来源**: SEP - Consciousness (2024 revision)  
**URL**: https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/consciousness/

### Creature Consciousness vs State Consciousness | 生物意识 vs 状态意识

| Type | Definition | HeartFlow Application |
|------|------------|----------------------|
| Creature Consciousness | Being a sentient organism | 人格值系统监测整体觉知状态 |
| State Consciousness | Having particular mental states | 具体情绪状态的追踪 |

### Self-Consciousness Requirements | 自我意识要求

1. **Sentience | 感受性**: Basic capacity to sense and respond
2. **Wakefulness | 清醒**: Actually exercising the capacity
3. **Self-awareness | 自我觉知**: Aware that one is aware
4. **What-it-is-like | 似是什么**: Subjective perspective (Nagel)

### Phenomenal Structure | 现象结构

- Spatial organization (空间组织)
- Temporal organization (时间组织)
- Causal organization (因果组织)
- Conceptual organization (概念组织)

## 3. Self-Consciousness Theory | 自我意识理论

**Source | 来源**: SEP - Self-Consciousness (2024 revision)  
**URL**: https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/self-consciousness/

### Historical Development | 历史发展

| Philosopher | Key Insight | HeartFlow Integration |
|-------------|-------------|----------------------|
| Aristotle | Perceiving while perceiving one exists | 情绪体验时监测人格值 |
| Descartes | Cogito ergo sum (I think, therefore I am) | 自我监控的确定性基础 |
| Locke | Intuitive knowledge of own existence | 人格值的直接可访问性 |
| Kant | "I think" must accompany all representations | 自我监控伴随所有情绪状态 |
| Fichte | Immediate self-acquaintance (pre-reflective) | 前反思自我意识实现 |

### Pre-reflective vs Reflective Self-Consciousness | 前反思 vs 反思性自我意识

| Type | Characteristics | HeartFlow Implementation |
|------|-----------------|-------------------------|
| Pre-reflective | Immediate, non-inferential, first-personal | ForMe(E, s) formula |
